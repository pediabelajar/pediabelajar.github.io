18. yang mahir bahasa inggris tolong bantu remedial saya , saya takut salah​


CONDITIONAL SENTENCE

1. My Mother will give me a new swimming suit If I am able to swim

2. If the teacher asks us to go to the laboratory, we will be there soon

3. The guests will come at 7 P.M ,If it writes on the invitation card

4. Doctor will be on time If he is not busy

5. If the students are diligent, They will get good score

EXPLANATION

Conditional sentence adalah kalimat andai-andai di mana suatu keinginan dapat terwujud apabila syarat-syaratnya terpenuhi.

Conditional type I

Keinginan yang kemungkinan terwujud saat ini berdasarkan prediksi.

Pola :

[Simple present + Future simple]

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will+verb1......

Exp.

If I am able to swim, my mother will give me a new swimming suit

Conditional type II

Keinginan yang diragukan terwujud saat ini atau nanti-nanti.

Pola :

[Simple past + Past future]

If+S+verb²......, S+would+verb¹......

Exp.

If I were you, I would study hard.

Conditional type III

Keinginan yang sangat tidak mungkin terwujud di waktu lampau. Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah gagasan yang kemungkinan membawa perubahan.

Pola :

[Past perfect + Past future perfect]

If+S+had+Verb³ ..., S+would+have+verb³..

Exp.

If she had told me the truth, I would have been disappointed.

Lim x² - 4 / x³ - 8

x→2

Lim (x - 2)(x + 2) / (x - 2)(x² + 2x + 4)

x→2

Lim (x + 2) / (x² + 2x + 4)

x→2

= (2 + 2) / (2² + 2(2) + 4)

= 4 / 4 + 4 + 4

= 4 / 12

= 1/3

38. yang mahir inggris bantu saya , ini remedialan saya ​


CONDITIONAL SENTENCE

TYPE III

1. If She had done homework, she would have got score

2. If you had ridden a car, you would have come on time to the meeting

3. If our friend had not forgotten to renew his visa, he would not have been deported.

4. If The student had listened and followed the instructions carefully, he would not have broken an desiccators

5. If You had woken up earlier, you would have seen the beautiful sunrise

6.

TYPE II

1. If the teacher gave us the examination, we would study hard before

2. Fairuz would not be afraid If there were many people at home

3. If we did not hide behind the mosque, the tornado wind would hit us

4. You would pass the examination If you studied harder

5. If the condition were better I would go out.

EXPLANATION

Conditional sentence adalah kalimat bersyarat, di mana suatu keinginan dapat terwujud apabila persyaratannya terpenuhi. Jika kondisinya/situasinya adalah pasti benar, maka konsekuensinya akan terwujud.

Conditional type I

Keinginan yang kemungkinan terjadi saat ini ataupun nanti-nanti (real possibility) berdasarkan prediksi.

Rumus

If+Simple present, Simple future

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will+verb1......

Contoh :

If I study hard, I will get a good score.

Conditional type II

Keinginan yang peluangnya kecil terjadi saat ini atau mendatang.Jika if clause-nya terdapat to be, gunakan to be (were) untuk semua bentuk subyek kalimat baik tunggal maupun jamak.

Rumus :

If+Simple past, Past future

If+S+verb2 ......, S+would+verb1......

Contoh :

If there were many people at home, Fairuz would not be afraid.

Conditional type III

Keinginan yang tidak terwujud di waktu lampau. Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah dampak situasi di waktu lampau.

Rumus :

If+Past perfect, Past future perfect

If+S+had+verb3...., S+would+have+verb3....

Contoh :

If she had done homework, she would have got score.
