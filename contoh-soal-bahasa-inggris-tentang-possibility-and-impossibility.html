21. Penjelasan tentang possibility ini the past dan kasih contohnya.


We use past possibility to talk about things which have possibly happened in the past to cause the situation we are in.

For example,  John is late to work,he hasn’t arrived yet.  Nobody knows what has happened to him, so we start to ask about the possible things which have made him late.
There are many possible reasons why John is late:
He has got stuck in traffic
He has got lost
He has forgotten

Some of these possibilities could be very likely, some could be quite likely (50%), some could be unlikely.
To discuss how possible it is that these events have happened we use the modal verbs must have, should have, may have, might have, can’t have.

25. Pengertian dari show possibility and impossibility


show possibility = Menunjukkan kemungkinan
yaitu kebisaan atau kemampuan suatu hal atau seseorang melakukan atau menghadiri sesuatu dengan alasan mengapa bisa

Impossibility = Menunjukan ketidakmungkinan suatu hal atau seseorang melakukan atau menghadiri sesuatu dengan alasan mengapa hal tersebut tidak mungkinShow possibility
=> ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menggambarkan suatu kemungkinan.

Contoh:
It's possible for you to give me a letter before you go to Jakarta.
(Kemungkinan kamu bisa memberikanku sepucuk surat sebelum kamu pergi ke Jakarta)

Show Impossibility
Digunakan untuk menggambarkan ketidakmungkinan.

Contoh:
You can't possibly go to my house because it is raining outside now.
(Kamu tidak mungkin pergi ke rumahku karena diluar sedang hujan sekarang)
