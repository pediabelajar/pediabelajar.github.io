1. contoh condolence letter formal dalam bahasa inggris


December, 2017

Mr. Ananta Mulya.

CEO,

Woodsworth Textiles,

New Jersey.

Dear Ananta

I would like to express my sincere condolences for the unexpected death of your father. On behalf of the entire office, please accept our sympathy. I can only imagine what a difficult loss this will be for you and your family.

Though I did not know your father except for few occasions when I got a chance to know his kindness and great thoughts. Just in those few moments I was highly impressed by his views and ideas.

You must proud that god had blessed you with such a great personality. I understand that words are not enough to heel this great loss but you need to keep patience.

All the members of the team have sent their sincere sympathy to you and your family. Please pass on our commiserations to your family and friends.

May you all get some comfort in this difficult situation?

Sincere condolence

Radika Maurisha

Contoh Greeting Card

-Fungsi pertama yaitu kartu ucapan (greeting card) sebagai sarana untuk menyatakan atau mengungkapkan ucapan selamat (congratulation) terhadap beberapa situasi bahagia contohnya seperti haru ulang tahun, hari penikahan, selamat mendapat sebuah prestasi, hari besar agama sebagai tanda dan bentuk sosial untuk ikut merasakan bahagia saat yang lain merasa bahagia.

-Fungsi kedua kartu ucapan dalam bahasa ingggris yang biasanya ditujukan dalam rangka untuk ikut menunjukkan rasa simpati atas kejadian atau situasi yang kurang menyenangkan, seperti saat teman sedang sakit, sebgaai contoh ucapan yaitu get well soon “semoga cepat sembuh”.

28. contoh dialog tentang expressing sympathy dan expressing condolence


Lisa : Hi, Jennie. Why didn't you come to school yesterday?

Lisa : Oh! I am sorry to hear that. (contoh expressing condolences)

39. contoh kalimat condolence dan warning minimal 3


Condolence= to express sympathy.
Warning= peringatan.

Ex. Condolence:
1. I'm so sorry for the death of your beloved grandmother.
2. Please accept my deepest condolence for your loss.
3. We would like to express our condolence for you and your family.

Ex. Warning:
1. Do not enter this area, this is dangerous for your safety.
2. Don't touch the electric fences, ot might get you electrocuted.
3. Warning, it is prohibited for you to come here. Condolence  :
- I am very sorry to hear that
- we are saddened to hear of your sudden loss
- I was heartbroken by this sad news

Warning  :
- I am warning you no to get close to him. He's suspicious.
- Watch out!! Be careful on the way!
- Make sure you don't accept anything from a stranger, okay?
