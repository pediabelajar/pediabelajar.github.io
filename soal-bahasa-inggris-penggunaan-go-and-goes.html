4. bedanya goes dan gojelaskan!​


Jawaban:

go dipakai pada subjek i, you, they, we. sedangkan goes digunakan pada subjek he, she, it

Jawaban:

go untuk subject I You They We

goes untuk subject He She Its

untuk kalimat positif :

I/You/They/We + V1 + object

He/She/It + V1 + s/es + object

untuk kalimat negatif :

I/You/They/We + do not + V1 + object

He/She/It + does not + V1 + object

untuk kalimat tanya :

do + I/You/They/We + V1 + object + ?

does + He/She/It + V1 + object + ?

19. Ayu always milk before she goes to school TOLONG DIJAWAB YANG BENAR YA INI SOAL BAHASA INGGRIS KELAS 5


To complete the sentence, we need to use simple present verb. The reason why we need simple present tense is because of the frequency adverbs 'always'. Simple present tense is used when we're talking about our daily routines or something that is done repeatedly. We can also use simple present to state a fact or something that is already true.

Discussion

The sentence that needs to be completed is Ayu always ____ milk before she goes to school.

The pattern for simple present is 'subject + verb + time marker'. The verb changes depending on the subject used on the sentence.

For example:

- Nisa eats toast every morning.

- I drink coffee every morning.

- They play basketball every Tuesday.

- He watches TV every night.

- She always buys ice cream after school.

Answer

Ayu always drinks milk before she goes to school.

30. Sam, Mary and I ... to school onfoot every day.are gogoesis gogo​


Sam, Mary and I go to school on foot every day. Jawaban yang tepat merupakan kata kerja (verbs) go dengan makna menurut Oxford Learners Dictionaries berpindah dari satu tempat ke tempat lain atau makna sederhananya adalah pergi.

Pembahasan

Kalimat di atas merupakan Simple Present Tense yang menggunakan kata kerja dasar (base verbs). Kalimat dengan tenses present simple menunjukkan pendapat, fakta, maupun kegiatan/kebiasaan yang dilakukan secara berulang.

Pola kalimat:

Subjek jamak (You, They, We): S + V1 (base form) → You love to do yoga every evening.Subjek tunggal (I): S + V1 (base form) → I like to do yoga every evening.Subjek tunggal: (He, She (atau nama orang), It (atau nama benda)): S + V1 (s/es form) → Gemma likes to do yoga every evening.

31. sofia (goes/go) to school by herself jawaban ny apa ya goes atau go?​


Jawabannya adalah goes ✅

Sofia goes to school by herself

PEMBAHASAN Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan berulang/rutin, kebiasaan dan hal2 bersifat umum atau fakta.

Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + V1 (-s/es)

(-) S + do/does + not + V1

(?) Do/does + S + V1?

Catatan:Jika Subjek jamak menggunakan Verb-1 sajaTapi jika Subjek tunggal menggunakan Verb+es

KESIMPULAN

Sofia goes to school by herself

Sofia => Subject tunggal

Goes => Verb+es karena Subjek tunggal
