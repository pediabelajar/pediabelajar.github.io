3. Don't litter.

4. Don't be late for class.

5. Don't talk during the movie.

6. Don't give up.

7. Don't waste food.

9. Preserve natural resources: Conserve natural resources by using them efficiently and responsibly. Consider alternatives to non-renewable resources and support initiatives for conservation and preservation.

10. Get involved: Participate in local community initiatives, volunteer for environmental organizations, and support policies and regulations that aim to protect and improve the environment.

37. write three tips for exam with conditional sentences with imperatives!mohon bantuannya terimakasih


Conditional sentences with imperatives are zero conditionals which are used to give instructions. The conditional clause is written in present tense and the main clause is written with the imperative (without subject, starting with bare infinitive).

Kalimat kondisi menggunakan bentuk perintah adalah kalimat kondisi tipe 0 yang digunakan untuk memberikan instruksi/perintah. Klausa kondisi nya ditulis dalam pola present tense dan klausa utamanya ditulis dalam bentuk kalimat perintah (tanpa subjek, dimulai dengan kata kerja bentuk dasar).

Contoh zero conditional yang menggunakan imperative:

If you see Tom, tell him to return my book.If the cough gets worse, go and see a doctor.If Anna arrives early, ask her to prepare herself for the presentation.
Pembahasan

Contoh petunjuk atau nasihat untuk ujian:

If you find a difficult problem, move on and find an easier problem to solve first so as not to waste time.If you want to avoid brain fatigue, study the materials part by part over a period of time prior to the day of exam.If you think the examination is going to be difficult, give yourself enough time to prepare and study ahead.
