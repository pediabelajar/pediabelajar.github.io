34. deskripsi buku dalam bahsa inggris verbal dan nominal


Verbal Description (Deskripsi Verbal):

A book's verbal description refers to the narrative or textual content that conveys the essence and details of the book using words. This includes the plot summary, character development, themes, writing style, and any other literary elements that contribute to the overall story. In a verbal description, one would discuss the narrative structure, the progression of events, and the impact of the author's writing on the reader. It focuses on the linguistic and expressive aspects of the book, providing insights into the author's storytelling prowess and the emotional engagement it elicits

Nominal Description (Deskripsi Nominal):

On the other hand, a nominal description of a book involves providing information about its basic, concrete attributes such as the title, author, genre, publication date, and physical characteristics. This type of description is more factual and straightforward, offering a summary of the book's metadata. Nominal descriptions are concise and focus on the external, observable aspects of the book, making it easier for readers to identify and locate the book without delving into its content. It serves as a quick reference for those seeking basic information about the book..
