22. buatlah 6 soal pilihan ganda 5 option tentang if clause suggestion beserta jawabannya​


Jawaban:

Part 1 (If Clause – Intermediate Level)

Soal

Fill in the correct forms of the verbs in brackets, according to the type of the IF-sentence shown at the end!

1. If he _______ (EAT) everything he _______ (BE) ill. (3)

2. The police _______ (ARREST) him if they _______ (CATCH) him. (1)

3. What _______ (HAPPEN) if my parachute _______ (NOT OPEN)? (1)

Jawaban

1. If he HAD EATEN everything he WOULD HAVE BEEN ill. (3)

2. The police WILL ARREST him if they CATCH him. (1)

3. What WILL HAPPEN if my parachute DOESN’T OPEN? (1)

Part 2 (If Clause – Intermediate Level)

Soal

Fill in the correct form of the verb in brackets!

1. If Anne’s father gets seriously ill, they _______ (BRING) him to the hospital.

2. If I _______ (BE) you I would pay more attention in class.

3. They _______ (NOT GO) out if they don’t get a babysitter.

Jawaban

1. If Anne’s father gets seriously ill, they WILL BRING him to the hospital.

2. If I WAS/ WERE you I would pay more attention in class.

3. They WON’T GO out if they don’t get a babysitter.

Part 3 (If Clause – Intermediate Level)

Soal

Put the verbs in brackets into their correct tense!

1. The burglars _______ (NOT DO) so much damage if you had locked all the drawers.

2. If he had slipped, he _______ (FALL) 500 metres.

3. They would have forced their way into the house if I _______ (NOT CALL) for help.

Jawaban

1. The burglars WOULDN’T HAVE DONE so much damage if you had locked all the drawers.

2. If he had slipped, he WOULD HAVE FALLEN 500 metres.

3. They would have forced their way into the house if I HADN’T CALLED for help.

28. Berikan contoh expressing offers and suggestion


A. Suggestion

a. Definition

Suggestion (saran) adalah mengajukan suatu

rencana untuk dipertimbangkan atau memberikan/menyumbangkan suatu ide. Hal-hal yang disarankan dapat diterima atau ditolak. Suggestion bersifat abstrak, dan hal-hal yang bisa disarankan seperti solution (solusi), advice (nasehat), plan (rencana), dan idea (ide).

b. Expressions Suggestion :

- Let's ...

- Why don't we ...

- We could ...

- What about ...

- I suggest that ...

- You might want to change ...

- I think ...

- I don't think ...

c. Responding Suggestion :

Accepting Suggestion

- Yes, let's go

- Ok, I will

- It's a good idea

- Okay, if you say so

- Let's

Declining Suggestion

- No, thank you. I don't feel like going

- Sorry, I think ...

- No, let's just do something else

- No, let's just go somewhere else

- Sorry, I can't. I have another appointment

- No, sorry.

d. Example :

- Dialogue 1 :

A : Let's go to the beach this weekend!

B : It's a good idea. Let's go!

- Dialogue 2 :

A : What don't you study for the test instead of playing outside?

B : Okay, if you say so

- Dialogue 3 :

A : I think you should go apologize to her

B : Sorry, I can't. It's her fault

- Dialogue 4 :

A : You might want to change your mind

B : No, sorry, I've planned this for months

B. Offers

a. Definition

Offers (tawaran) adalah memberikan sesuatu kepada seseorang sebagai hadiah (gifts), pertukaran (trade) atau bantuan (help). Pada umumnya, offers bersifat nyata (konkrit), dan hal-hal yang dapat diberikan kepada seseorang sebagai offers seperti food (makanan), money (uang), solution (solusi), friendship (persahabatan), atau bargain (penawaran).

b. Expression Offers :

- May I ...

- Can I ...

- Shall I ...

- Would you ...

- How about I ...

c. Responding Offers :

Accepting Offers

- Yes, please. I really appreciate it

- Thank you, it's very kind of you

- Yes, please, that be lovely

- Yes, please, that would be very kind of you

- Thank you, I appreciate your help

Declining Offers

- It's okay, I can do it myself

- No, thank you

- No, thanks

- Don't worry, I will do it myself

- That's alright, I will manage it on my own

d. Example :

- Dialogue 1 :

A : Can I help you?

B : Yes, please, I really appreciate your help

- Dialogue 2 :

A : Could I offer you something?

B : Yes, please, I want a cup of tea

- Dialogue 3 :

A : Would you care for another cup of tea?

B : No, thank you.

- Dialogue 4 :

A : Can I clean the car for you?

B : It's okay, I can do it myself
