1. contoh deskripsi tentang PRAMUGARI dalam bahasa inggris.


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: job
Pembahasan:

A flight attendant or a stewardess is quite a popular dream job among female. The stewardesses works for the airline and travels everywhere based on the airline's destination. Their main job are to serve the passengers, keep them safe and provide other necessary services including helping the elder ones, controlling the situation inside the plane should there be a complaint etc. In terms of working hours, they definitely do not have a regular nine to five hours as they very often have to fly for long hours in international flight and stay for a night before continuing to the next one. However, it is said that the reward is quite appealing as they could choose to pick any flight in the airline destination for their holiday for free, not to mention the long leave as well, especially for the ones working for international airlines. It is certainly worth doing since their job is quite risky too when it comes to a plane hijack or accident, in which they have to prioritize the passengers' life instead of their own.

A. steril-stetoskop

D. kotor-kuman

E. bersih-bakteri

Jika sakit maka harus istirahat, jika lapar maka harus makan. Jawaban: C
