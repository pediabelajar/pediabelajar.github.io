Ya, dia sedang memainkan mainan robot. Perhatikan kata "adalah" dan "bermain". Itu adalah gabungan dari to be present tense dan verb-ing. Kami biasanya menemukannya dalam present continuous tense. Pada bab ini, kita akan mempelajari tentang verb-ing dalam present continuous tense. ketika kita sedang melakukan suatu aktivitas.

Kami menggunakan present continuous tense Present continuous tense menggunakan kata kerja.

Berikut rumus present continuous tense.Ada tiga bentuk present continuous tense.

Kita menggunakan “is” untuk subjek He, She, It.

Kita menggunakan "am" untuk subjek /.

Kita menggunakan “are” untuk subjek Mereka. Kami, Anda.

22. bahasa Inggris hal 29-30 membuat pertanyaan serta jawabannya dalam bahasa inggris, masing-masing conversation buat 5 pertanyaan dengan jawabannya


Jawaban:

Conversation 1: Travel Plans

1. Question: Are you planning a vacation this year?

Answer: Yes, I'm planning a trip to Europe in the summer.

2. Question: Where in Europe are you thinking of visiting?

Answer: I'm considering Italy and France for this vacation.

3. Question: What's your favorite part about traveling?

Answer: I love experiencing new cultures and trying different cuisines.

4. Question: How do you usually prepare for a trip?

Answer: I research the destinations, book accommodations, and make an itinerary.

5. Question: Have you ever had any memorable travel adventures?

Answer: Yes, I once went backpacking through Southeast Asia, and it was an incredible journey.

Conversation 2: Hobbies

1. Question: What are your favorite hobbies?

Answer: I enjoy painting, hiking, and playing the guitar in my free time.

2. Question: How did you get into painting?

Answer: I started painting as a child and continued to explore it as a hobby.

3. Question: Do you find your hobbies relaxing?

Answer: Absolutely, my hobbies are a great way to relax and unwind.

4. Question: Have you ever turned one of your hobbies into a profession?

Answer: No, I prefer to keep my hobbies as enjoyable pastimes.

5. Question: What's the most challenging part of pursuing your hobbies?

Answer: Sometimes finding the time for them can be a challenge, especially when work gets busy.

Conversation 3: Food Preferences

1. Question: What's your favorite type of cuisine?

Answer: I'm a big fan of Italian food, especially pasta and pizza.

2. Question: Do you have any dietary restrictions or preferences?

Answer: I'm a vegetarian, so I avoid meat in my diet.

3. Question: Are there any dishes you enjoy cooking yourself?

Answer: I love making homemade lasagna; it's one of my specialties.

4. Question: Have you ever tried cooking international dishes?

Answer: Yes, I've experimented with making sushi, and it turned out pretty well.

5. Question: What's your go-to comfort food?

Answer: A warm bowl of chicken soup is my ultimate comfort food on a cold day.
