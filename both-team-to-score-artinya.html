Jawaban:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Maria a.k.a M and Rizal a.k.a R

Known : M = R + 46

M = 3R

Asked : How many points did Maria score?

Answer : M = R + 46, it means M - R = 46

So, we use substitution

M - R = 46

3R - R = 46

2R = 46

R = 46/2

R = 23

M = 3R

= 3 × 23

= 69

int midScore;

scanf ( "%i" );

atau dapat dibaca " scan variable integer "

Setelah itu kita arahkan variable integer ini mau kita masukan ke variable apa. Dalam kasus ini kita ingin memasukan nilainya ke variable assignment. Untuk itu kita tulis:

scanf ( "%i", &assignmentScore );

lalu untuk memeriksa apakah pengimputan nilai telah berhasil, kita bisa check dengan mencetak ketiga varible yang ada. Contohnya ada pada Screenshot yang sudah terlampir ⬇

34. Medina and Adi play chess, they agree to play 6 times, and have the following rules: Win given a score = 15 Lose given a score =-3 Draw given s score = 1 If Medina wins 3 times, and draw 2 times, then the total value obtained by both of them is ....


Let's calculate the total score obtained by Medina and Adi based on the given information:

Medina's Wins: 3 times (15 points each) = 45 points

Medina's Draws: 2 times (1 point each) = 2 points

Now, let's calculate Adi's score:

Adi's Wins: 6 - (Medina's Wins + Medina's Draws) = 6 - (3 + 2) = 1 time (15 points)

Adi's Losses: 6 - (Medina's Wins + Medina's Draws + Adi's Wins) = 6 - (3 + 2 + 1) = 0 times

Now we can calculate the total score obtained by both Medina and Adi:

Total Score = Medina's Score + Adi's Score

Total Score = (Medina's Wins + Medina's Draws) + (Adi's Wins + Adi's Losses)

Total Score = (45 + 2) + (15 + 0)

Total Score = 47 + 15

Total Score = 62

So, the total value obtained by both Medina and Adi is 62 points.
