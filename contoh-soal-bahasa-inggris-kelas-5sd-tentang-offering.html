8. tugas bahasa Inggris kelas 12 tentang offering help


Menjawab tugas bahasa Inggris kelas 12 tentang materi offering help adalah sebagai berikut.


3. You work in a tour agency. You see a young gentleman enter your office awkwardly. Offer your service and try to convince him to take one of your holiday packages.

Employee: Is there anything I can help you with, Sir? We are currently still in the vibes of Thanksgiving, so the black friday discount still valid. The last day will be tomorrow.

Customer: Yes, I’m actually planning for a trip to Japan with my wife.

Employee: You may check this package out, sir. With the black friday discount you’ll get 30% off from the normal price. It’s a package for 10 days trip in the 4 stars hotel. The facility is very competitive comparing to other travel agencies since comfort is our priority.

Customer: Hmm.. I’m quite interested with this one.



4. A friend is absent because she is sick. You visit her this afternoon. Your friend needs your help to communicate with the teacher about an assignment that she hasn’t finished yet. Offer her a help.

You: Hey, Lala how do you feel?

Lala: I still need to have some bed rest the doctor said.

You: I see, I hope you’ll get better soon. Oh by the way, what about your English task?

Lala: That’s the problem. I still haven’t finished it and the deadline is tomorrow right? I’m so worried about it.

You: Don’t worry Lala. I will take care of it. Let me talk to Ms. Lidya about your current situation, and I’m sure she will understand.

Lala: Thanks a lot! You are truly the person I can rely on.

You: No worries, a friend in need, is a friend indeed.


5. You want to go to the movie this weekend. You ask several friends to go with you. Two of your friends cannot make up their minds. Offer to treat them so that they can go with you.

You: Hey guys, I have 3 free movie tickets. Would you like to join me?

Anna: What’s the movie?

You: It’s Insidious 3.

Luna: OMG, I hate horror!

Anna: I have watched it last week with my brother.

You: Guys, please….. Okay how about I will treat you all lunch after the movie in Sushi Tei?

Luna: Hmmm sounds good. How about you Anna?

Anna: Well, maybe I’ll come.

You: Yasss.. Thanks guys. See you tomorrow!

12. Contoh dialog Offering korupsi bahasa Inggris


A: “Hey, have you seen the latest news?” (Hai, apakah kau sudah melihat berita terakhir?)

B: “Not really. I haven’t got the time to check the news lately. What has happened?” (Belum. Saya belum memiliki waktu melihat berita akhir-akhir ini. Apa yang telah terjadi?)

A: “There was another politician who got caught in a corruption case. I heard that he corrupted almost 15 million dollars!” (Ada politisi lain lagi yang tertangkap dalam kasus korupsi. Saya dengar bahwa ia mengkorupsi hampir 15 juta dolar!)

B: “Oh my God. That’s horrible. How could someone do that while knowing there’s still children living in poverty in this country?” (Ya ampun. Itu mengerikan. Bagaimana bisa seseorang melakukan itu sambil mengetahui bahwa masih ada anak kecil di negara ini yang hidup miskin?)

A: “I agree one hundred percent. All of these corruptor deserves a lifetime in jail.” (Saya setuju sepenuhnya. Semua koruptor ini pantas dipenjara seumur hidup)

B: “That’s right! I could never imagine doing such a thing myself.” (Itu benar! Saya tidak bisa membayangkan diri sendiri melakukan hal seperti itu)

17. Contoh 5 dialog bahasa inggris suggestion dan offering


Offer adalah sebuqah eksperesi yang diungkapkan tentang keinginan untuk melakukan sesuatu
Suggestion ialah suatu ide yang diberikan kepada seseorang agar ide tersebut  dilakukan.
 
Contoh-contoh offer:
Can I ………………………….. help you?
Shall I …………………………. Get you some drink?
Would like ……………………….  A cup of tea?
How about ……………………… some cookies?
 

Dialog : 

Waitress : Hi, can I help you?
Me          : yes please, I'd like fried rice with meat slices on it.
Waitress : How about trying our new appetizer menu, tempura shrimp with                        barbeque sauce?
Me : That would be great, I'd like some of it. 
Waitress : Shall I get you some drink too?
