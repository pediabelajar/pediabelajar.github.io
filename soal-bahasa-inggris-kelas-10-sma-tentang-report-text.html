Nanotechnology

What is nanotechnology? Technology is the activity or study of using scientific knowledge for practical purposes in industry, farming, medicine, business etc. Hence, nanotechnology means the study of the scientific application of nanoparticles. Nanoparticles are very, very small in size and they are measured in terms of nanometres (nm). A nanometre is one-billionth of a metre.  

To visualise its size let us go down from the length of a metre. We all know what a metrelength is. One tenth of this metre is a decimeter. One hundredth of a metre is a centimetre (cm). A millimetre is (mm) one thousandth of a metre. A micro-metre is one millionth of a metre. Allthese are still huge compared to a nanometre. A nanometre is one-billionth of a metre. Such is the smallness of these nanoparticles. For example, water consists of hydrogen atoms and oxygen atoms. Two atoms of hydrogen atoms combine with one atom of oxygenatom to form one water molecule (H2O). Similarly nature has perfected the science of manufacturing matter with the help of molecules. For instance, our bodies are assembled in a specific manner from millions of living cells. Cells are nature’s nanomachines.

Some of the applications of nanotechnology have already startled us. There is a substance called sunscreen lotion. It is applied on the skin to prevent it from being burnt by the sun. Now-a-days, this substance is manufactured by using nanoparticles. With the result, no tinge is formed on the skin. Previously it was not so. As soon as the sunscreen lotion was applied on the skin, a white tinge formed on the skin. Similarly, a new type of glass is manufactured using nano-technology called active glass. These glasses have the quality of polarising ultraviolet rays i.e., arranging ultra-violet rays in a defi nite direction. This has enabled many people working with computers to protect their eyes from some harmful rays. Nanotechnology is also used in cloth manufacturing. Some clothes are coated with nanoparticles of zinc oxide. They protect the clothes from getting stained. Some clothes have nanoparticles in the form of little hairs or whiskers that help repel water and other materials making the clothing stain-resistant.

You might have seen heat-resistant and water-resistant watches. They are called so because they do not allow heat to disturb their function or water to enter the watch. Similarly we have scratch-resistant glasses. The glass is coated with aluminium silicate nanoparticles that preventthe surfaces from chipping and scratching. Many of the glass surfaces in the car belong to this category.

You might have observed doctors dressing the wounds. What they do is, they apply medicine and bandage the wounds using cotton clothes. Now-a-days they are using bandage clothes coated with nanoparticles of silver. It is enough if these clothes are wound over the wounds. They kill the harmful germs and help heal the wounds.

Nanotechnology has affected the fi eld of sports and games. For example, tennis rackets and tennis balls are made of nanomaterials. Tennis rackets made of nanomaterials mixed with graphite are lighter but at the same time stronger than steel rackets. Similarly producing tennis balls using nanotechnology is of best quality. Likewise swimming pools are cleaned and made germfree using nanotechnology cleaners.

26. Bahasa inggris kelas 11 SMA. Anak SMA jawab ini ya!Explain the social function and generic structure of report text!Jago Bahasa Inggris SMA jawab ini ya!


Social Function of a Report Text 
The purpose of a report text is to present information about something generally. It generally describes the way things are with reference to arrange of natural, manmade and social phenomenon in our environment, such as: mammals, the planets, rocks, plants, countries of region, cultures, transportations, and so on

Generic structures
• Title
A title of a report text indicates the topic of the text and it is a very general thing.
• General Classification
A general classification is a part that state classification of general aspect of things, such as: animals, public places, plants, etc. It will be discussed in general.
• Description
This part gives describing of the things which will be discussed in detail, in terms of: parts (and their function), qualities, habits and behavior.

1. Definisi dan fungsi report text.

“Report is a text which presents information about something, as it is.
