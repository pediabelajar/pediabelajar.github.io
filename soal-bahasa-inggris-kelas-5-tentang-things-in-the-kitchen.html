26. Things in the kitchen are


Jawaban:

refrigerator, cooking utensils, plates, forks, spoons, stove, cabin, kitchen spices, pans, Teflon, oven, frying pan,etc..

Jawaban:

In the kitchen, you can find various items and appliances that are commonly found in a typical kitchen. Here are some examples:

1. Stove - used for cooking food by providing heat.

2. Oven - used for baking, roasting, and grilling food.

3. Refrigerator - used for keeping food and beverages cool and fresh.

4. Sink - used for washing dishes, fruits, and vegetables.

5. Cabinets - used for storing kitchen utensils, cookware, and food items.

6. Countertop - a flat surface for preparing and organizing food.

7. Cutting board - used for chopping and slicing ingredients.

8. Pots and pans - used for cooking on the stove or in the oven.

9. Utensils - includes knives, spoons, forks, and other tools used for eating and cooking.

10. Plates, bowls, and glasses - used for serving and eating meals.

11. Blender - used for blending and pureeing ingredients.

12. Toaster - used for toasting bread and bagels.

13. Microwave - used for quickly heating and cooking food.

14. Mixing bowls - used for combining ingredients when baking or cooking.

15. Bakeware - includes baking sheets, cake pans, and muffin tins for baking purposes.

These are just a few examples of what you can find in a kitchen. The items and appliances can vary depending on individual preferences and cooking styles.
