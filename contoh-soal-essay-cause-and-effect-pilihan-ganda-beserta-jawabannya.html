6. contoh soal pilihan ganda bahasa inggris cause and effect


Cause and Effect

Anna: Hey Lulu, I recognize that your face is looking brighter and your look slimmer! What’s the secret?

Lulu: Well, thanks for noticing Ann. For the past months I have been working out regularly at least three times a week. Thanks to yoga!

Anna: Wow, sounds very interesting.

Lulu: Yes, but not only it. I also have been taking care of my food. Due to my lactose intolerant, I try to avoid dairy as much as possible. And it works for me!

Anna: I’m very inspired Ann! As a result of my laziness and eating too much junk food, I gained 5 kilograms in a month! I need to get back in shape and do more workout.

Lulu: Yes, I support you!

Simak dialog di atas untuk menjawab soal-soal pilihan ganda berikut.

1. Based on the dialog, these are signal words mentioned. Except…

A. Thanks to

B. Because of

C. Due to

D. As a result of

2. “As a result of my laziness and eating too much junk food, I gained 5 kilograms in a month!”

Which one is the effect?

A. Becoming lazy

B. Eating too much junk food

C. Gained 5 kilograms in a month

D. Lose 5 kilograms in a month

3. What is the cause that Lulu’s face is looking brighter and she looks slimmer?

A. Yoga three times a week

B. Eating junk food

C. Drinking more dairy

D. Gain 5 kilograms

Pembahasan

Untuk mengungkapkan cause and effect dalam bahasa Inggris, kita menggunakan signal words berikut:

due tobecause ofowing tothanks toas a consequence ofas a result ofbecausesinceasfor

Nah, untuk menunjukan cause and effect dalam kalimat ada dua jenis yakni yang diikuti kata benda (followed by noun) dan yang diikuti kata kerja (followed by verb).

Followed by noun

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause

Due to Jen’s laziness, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: Due toCause: Jen’s lazinessEffect: She didn’t pass the test

He was sick as a consequence of his ignorance.

Signal phrase: As a consequenceCause: His ignoranceEffect: He was sick

Followed by verb

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause

Because Jen was lazy, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: BecauseCause: Jen was lazyEffect: She didn’t pass the Math test

As he ignored his health, he will be sick,

Signal word: AsCause: He ignoredEffect: He will be sick

11. contoh cause effect and effect cause​


Jawaban :

Contoh 1

I was lost so I opened Google Maps (Saya tersesat sehingga saya membuka Google Maps)

Cause : I was lost

Effect : I opened Google Maps

Conjunction : so

Contoh 2

The journey was quite quick because the road was clear. (perjalanan cukup cepat karena jalanan bersih dari hambatan )

Cause : the road was clear.

Effect : the journey was quite quick

Conjunction : because

Contoh 3

As it is raining again, I will have to stay at home. (karena hujan sedang turun lagi, saya akan tinggal di rumah)

Cause : it is raining again.

Effect : I will have to stay at home.

Conjunction : as

Contoh 4

Since she had not paid the rent,she was told to vacate the flat. (Karena dia belum membayar sewa, dia diberitahu untuk mengosongkan ruangan.)

Cause : she had not paid the rent.

Effect : she was told to vacate the flat.

Conjunction : since

Contoh 5

When the ocean is extremely polluted, coral reefs die..

Cause : the ocean is extremely polluted.

Effect : coral reefs die.

Conjunction : when

Hal yang perlu diperhatikan adalah jika kata hubung terletak di awal kalimat, kita perlu menggunakan tanda baca koma. Akan tetapi, jika kata hubungnya berada di tengah, kita tidak perlu menggunakan koma.

As it was getting late, We decided to book into a hotel.

(Karena sudah larut, kami memutuskan untuk memesan hotel.)

You can go first as you're the oldest.

(Anda boleh duluan karena Anda yang tertua.)

1. Question: What causes acid rain and what is its impact on the environment?

Answer: Acid rain is caused by the emission of pollutant gases such as sulfur dioxide and nitrogen oxides into the atmosphere that react with rainwater. As a result, acid rain can damage soil, water, and plants, and can negatively impact human and animal health.

2. Question: What causes global warming and what is its impact on the world's climate?

Answer: Global warming is caused by an increase in greenhouse gas emissions such as carbon dioxide. The result is an increase in the Earth's average temperature, leading to climate changes like increased intensity of extreme weather and rising sea levels.

3. Question: What causes deforestation and what is its impact on biodiversity?

Answer: Deforestation is often caused by the clearing of forests for agriculture, plantations, or mining. The result is that deforestation can reduce the natural habitats of forest species, threatening biodiversity.

4. Question: What causes forest fires and what is their impact on air quality?

Answer: Forest fires are often caused by human activities, such as logging and land clearing by burning. The result is that forest fires can produce smoke and particles that damage air quality and impact human health.

5. Question: What causes water pollution and what is its impact on marine life?

Answer: Water pollution is often caused by the disposal of industrial or household waste into water. The result is that water pollution can damage water ecosystems, reduce water quality, and negatively impact marine life.

6. Question: What causes obesity and what is its impact on health?

Answer: Obesity is usually caused by unhealthy eating habits and lack of physical activity. The result is that obesity can increase the risk of various diseases, such as diabetes, heart disease, and certain types of cancer.

7. Question: What causes the increase in the use of renewable energy and what is its impact on the environment?

Answer: The increase in the use of renewable energy is often due to concerns about climate change and the depletion of fossil fuel sources. The result is that the use of renewable energy can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions and has a lower environmental impact compared to fossil energy.

8. Question: What causes the decline in bee populations and what is its impact on agriculture?

Answer: The decline in bee populations is often caused by the use of pesticides, climate change, and loss of habitat. The result is that a decline in bee populations can reduce pollination, which is important for agriculture and food production.

9. Question: What causes the increase in the use of digital technology and what is its impact on society?

Answer: The increase in the use of digital technology is often due to technological advancements and the need to increase efficiency and productivity. The result is that the use of digital technology can affect the way we work, learn, and interact with others.

10. Question: What causes stress and what is its impact on mental health?

Answer: Stress can be caused by various factors, such as work pressure, financial problems, or personal issues. The result is that stress can affect mental and physical health, including an increased risk of depression, anxiety, and heart disease.
