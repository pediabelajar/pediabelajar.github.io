7. percakapan 10 tentang conditional centense dalam bahasa inggris


Joe: Hey, Alex, what's up?
Alex: Nothing... you know, I really like this girl, and I want to ask her out.
Joe: So, what's the problem?
Alex: Well, what if I would ask her and she said no? 
Joe: Why would she say no?
Alex: Well, maybe if I were in a band, she'd go out with me. Do you want to start a band?
Joe: No. You don't even play an instrument. That's crazy.
Alex: Yeah. Well, I think her last boyfriend had a really cool car. Maybe if I had a nicer car, she'd say yes. 
Joe: You're lucky to have a car.
Alex: I don't know. She's really pretty. If I were better-looking, she might say yes. 
Joe: You look fine. You can't worry about that.
Alex: Well, if I had more money, I could take her to a really nice restaurant. Hey, could I borrow some money? 
Joe: No way, then she'd expect you to take her to nice restaurants all the time.
Alex: Yeah, you're right.
Joe: Look, maybe if you were so nervous about it, she'd say yes. Just be yourself and ask her out. If she likes you, she'll go out with you. 
Alex: Yeah, you're right. I'll call her and ask her out.
Joe: Great. I'll see you later. Let me know what happens.
Alex: Okay... Wait Joe. Wait. What if she will say yes, but she really doesn't like me? Joe? Joe?

18. Bahasa Inggris kelas 12 SMA Conditional text dan Procedure Text.Jawab ya no.6 jangan asal!Baca soalnya yang teliti dan Jago bahasa Inggris ya!


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS

MATERI : CONDITIONAL SENTENCE

KELAS : 9

KODE SOAL : 9.5

KODE KATEGORISASI : 9.5.4


PEMBAHASAN

================

Untuk melengkapi kalimat di atas jawaban yang tepat adalah...

(A) will get

Alasannya adalah karena kalimat di atas bentuk CS Type 1 dimana setelah "if you do" disini kata kerjnya menggunakan V1, So conditional nya harus will + V1 "will get"


Karena rumus conditional type 1

If + simple present, conditional (will+V1)

Kalimat ini adalah contoh kalimat First Conditional/Conditional Sentence Type 1, diketahui dari tense yang digunakan setelah kata "if", yaitu Simple Present Tense (you do this test well).

Maka klausa setelahnya(setelah tanda koma), menggunakan Simple Future Tense. (you will get a good mark).

Rumus First Conditional: If + (simple present tense), (simple future tense).

JAWABANNYA: A

36. Apa contoh type 1 conditional dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

Conditional Sentence type 1 digunakan ketika kita ingin mengungkapkan sesuatu yang memiliki kemungkin akan terjadi di masa yang akan datang.

Rumusnya

If+simple present, simple future (will)

Contoh:

"If i come home early, i will get some rest"

"Jika aku pulang lebih awal, aku akan beristirahat"

Nah jadi maksudnya jika kamu pulang lebih awal, kemungkinan untuk bisa beristirahat itu ada dan bisa terjadi.

CONTOH

If it rains, you will get wet.

You will get wet if it rains.

If Sally is late again I will be mad.

I will be mad if Sally is late again.

If you don't hurry, you will miss the bus.

You will miss the bus if you don't hurry.
