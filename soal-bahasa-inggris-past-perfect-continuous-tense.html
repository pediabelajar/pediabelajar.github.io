4. apa perbedaan past perfect continuous tense dan present perfect continuous ?


bedanya hanya pada waktu terjadinya saja. 
present perfect continous, pembicara menceritakan bahwa kejadiannya masih berlangsung. misalnya 
" I've been living in Indonesia since 1990." 
dari sini menyatakan bahwa pembicara telah tinggal sejak tahun 1990 dan sekarang masih tinggal di indonesia, sampai waktu yang tidak ditentukan. 

sedangkan pada past perfect continous, pembicaranya membicarkan kejadian yang terjadinya pada masa lalu dan kejadian tersebut telah berakhir. biasanya pada tenses jenis ini dilengkapi dengan kalimat lain yang mendukung kejadian tersebut. misalnya 
" we had been talking for three minutes before her boy friend came." 
dari kalimat tersebut terlihat bahwa kejadian berbincang tersebut telah terjadi selama tiga menit sebelum kejadian datangnya boy friend. 

past perfect hanya menekankan pada lamanya durasi kejadian

rumus:

past perfect continuous : S + Had + been + V1 ing

present perect continuous: S + Have + been + V1 ing

Berikut ini adalah contoh kalimat-kalimat yang menggunakan kata "invite" dalam berbagai bentuk tense dan voice dalam bahasa Inggris:

Present Tense (Simple Present):

- Active: She invites her friends to the party.

- Passive: The guests are invited to the party.

Past Tense (Simple Past):

- Active: He invited me to his wedding last year.

- Passive: I was invited to his wedding last year.

Present Continuous:

- Active: They are inviting guests for the charity event.

- Passive: The guests are being invited for the charity event.

Past Continuous:

- Active: We were inviting people to join our club when it started raining.

- Passive: People were being invited to join our club when it started raining.

Present Perfect:

- Active: The company has invited a renowned speaker for the conference.

- Passive: A renowned speaker has been invited for the conference.

Past Perfect:

- Active: She had invited all her relatives to the family reunion before she left.

- Passive: All her relatives had been invited to the family reunion before she left.

Future Tense (be going to):

- Active: We are going to invite our neighbors to the barbecue party next weekend.

- Passive: Our neighbors are going to be invited to the barbecue party next weekend.

Modal Verb 1 (can):

- Active: Can you invite Sarah to the meeting tomorrow?

- Passive: Can Sarah be invited to the meeting tomorrow?

Modal Verb 2 (should):

- Active: You should invite your parents to the graduation ceremony.

- Passive: Your parents should be invited to the graduation ceremony.

13. Buatlah masing masing satu kalimat active voice dari1. present perfect continuous tense 2. past perfect continuous tense 3. future perfect tense 4. past future perfect tense 5. future perfect continuous tense 6. past future perfect continuous tense.lalu ubah kalimat ke passive voice ​


Jawaban:

1) Active and Passive Voice "Present Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice: Angelina has been practicing her English ability.

Passive Voice: Practicing English has been being ability by Angelina.

2) Active and Passive Voice "Past Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice: Sam was tired because he had been jogging.

Passive Voice: Jogging had been being tired by Sam.

3) Active and Passive Voice "Future Perfect Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Will + Have+V3.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Will + Have + Been + V3 + by...

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice: She will have won the first prize.

Passive Voice: The first prize will have been won by her.

4) Active and Passive Voice "Past Future Perfect Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Would/Should/Could/May/Might/ Must + Have + V3/Past Participile.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Would + Have + Been + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice:

I would have been eating dinner with my friends today. Angelina would have been gone to the salon after lunch. I would have been studying about martial arts.

Passive Voice:

Dinner would have been eaten today by my friends and meSalon would have been gone after lunch by Angelina.Martial arts would have been studied about by me.

5) Active and Passive Voice "Future Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Will + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Subject + To be + Going to + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Will + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Subject + To be + Going to + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice:

My friend will have been graduating from University of Indonesia by the end of this month.We will have been singing a few song at the party.I will have been listening a music tonight.Emily have been feeding the cat.

Passive Voice:

University of Indonesia will have been being graduated by the end of this month by my friend.A few song will have been being sung at the party by us. A music will have been being listened by me.The cat will have been being fed by Emily.

6) Active and Passive Voice "Past Future Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Would + Have + Been + V1-ing/Present Participle.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Would + Have + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice:

I would have been driving the car.Angelina and her family would have been living at their new house if its construction had finished.We would have been leaving on vacation in Bali island.They would have been playing martial arts.I would have been sleeping to finish my homework.We would have been going to the cinemas tonight.She would have been eating health food because it is good for health.We would have been going to the museum.I would have been learning Japanese language.We would have been learning Thai language.We would have been going to martial arts.

Passive Voice:

The car would have been being driven by me.Their new house would have been being left if its construction had finished by Angelina and her family.Bali island would have been being left by us.Martial arts would have been being played by them.My homework would have been being slept by me.Health food would have been being eaten because it is good for health by her.The museum would have been being gone by us.Japanese language would have been being learnt by me.Thai language would have been being learnt by us.Martial arts would have been being gone by us.

34. kak tolong kasih tau contoh kalimat positif negatif dan introgatifnya passive voice dari Present continuous tense, Present perfect tense, Present perfect continuous tense, Simple past tense, Past continuous tense, Past perfect tense, Past perfect continuous, dunggg


Jawaban:

Berikut adalah contoh kalimat positif, negatif, dan interrogatif dalam bentuk passive voice dari beberapa tense yang Anda sebutkan:

1. Present Continuous Tense (sekarang sedang terjadi):

- Positif: The cake is being baked by my sister. (Adik perempuan saya sedang memanggang kue.)

- Negatif: The book is not being read by the students. (Buku tidak sedang dibaca oleh para siswa.)

- Interrogatif: Is the car being washed by the mechanic? (Apakah mobil sedang dicuci oleh montir?)

2. Present Perfect Tense (terjadi di masa lampau namun memiliki dampak di masa sekarang):

- Positif: The house has been renovated by the new owners. (Rumah telah direnovasi oleh pemilik baru.)

- Negatif: The report hasn't been submitted by the team yet. (Laporan belum diserahkan oleh tim.)

- Interrogatif: Have the invitations been sent by the event organizer? (Apakah undangan telah dikirim oleh penyelenggara acara?)

3. Present Perfect Continuous Tense (terjadi di masa lalu dan masih berlanjut hingga sekarang):

- Positif: The project has been being worked on by the team for several months. (Proyek telah dikerjakan oleh tim selama beberapa bulan.)

- Negatif: The garden hasn't been being watered regularly. (Taman tidak selalu disiram secara teratur.)

- Interrogatif: How long has the patient been being treated by the doctor? (Berapa lama pasien telah dirawat oleh dokter?)

4. Simple Past Tense (terjadi di masa lalu):

- Positif: The movie was watched by millions of people. (Film tersebut ditonton oleh jutaan orang.)

- Negatif: The letter wasn't sent by John. (Surat itu tidak dikirim oleh John.)

- Interrogatif: Was the game played by the children yesterday? (Apakah permainan itu dimainkan oleh anak-anak kemarin?)

5. Past Continuous Tense (sedang terjadi di masa lampau):

- Positif: The car was being repaired by the mechanic when I arrived. (Mobil sedang diperbaiki oleh montir saat saya tiba.)

- Negatif: The phone wasn't being used by anyone when it rang. (Telepon tidak sedang digunakan oleh siapapun saat berdering.)

- Interrogatif: What were you doing when the accident happened? (Apa yang sedang kamu lakukan ketika kecelakaan terjadi?)

6. Past Perfect Tense (terjadi sebelum suatu waktu di masa lampau):

- Positif: The package had been delivered by the courier before I got home. (Paket telah dikirim oleh kurir sebelum saya pulang.)

- Negatif: The task hadn't been completed by the deadline. (Tugas belum selesai sebelum batas waktu.)

- Interrogatif: Had the tickets been booked by the travel agent when you called? (Apakah tiket sudah dipesan oleh agen perjalanan ketika kamu menelepon?)

7. Past Perfect Continuous Tense (terjadi sebelum suatu waktu di masa lampau dan masih berlanjut):

- Positif: The team had been working on the project for weeks before they finally completed it. (Tim telah bekerja pada proyek tersebut selama berbulan-bulan sebelum akhirnya menyelesaikannya.)

- Negatif: The plants hadn't been being watered regularly before they withered. (Tanaman tidak selalu disiram secara teratur sebelum layu.)

- Interrogatif: How long had they been waiting for the bus before it arrived? (Berapa lama mereka telah menunggu bus sebelum bus itu tiba?)
