5. contoh announcement dalam bahasa Inggris beserta artinya​


Jawaban:

Contoh Announcement:

1. Attention, students! The school library will be closed for renovations starting next Monday. We apologize for the inconvenience and will notify you once it reopens.

Artinya:

Perhatian, siswa-siswi! Perpustakaan sekolah akan ditutup untuk renovasi mulai Senin depan. Kami mohon maaf atas ketidaknyamanan ini dan akan memberitahu Anda ketika perpustakaan kembali dibuka.

2. Ladies and gentlemen, please be informed that there will be a power outage in the building tomorrow from 9 AM to 1 PM for maintenance purposes. Kindly make necessary arrangements and apologies for any inconvenience caused.

Artinya:

Hormatilah para tamu, mohon diberitahukan bahwa akan ada pemadaman listrik di gedung ini besok mulai pukul 9 pagi hingga pukul 1 siang untuk tujuan pemeliharaan. Mohon membuat pengaturan yang diperlukan dan mohon maaf atas segala ketidaknyamanan yang terjadi.

3. Attention, passengers! Flight MH123 to London has been delayed by two hours due to unforeseen circumstances. We apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your patience. Further updates will be provided shortly.

Artinya:

Perhatian, penumpang! Penerbangan MH123 ke London mengalami penundaan selama dua jam karena keadaan yang tidak terduga. Kami mohon maaf atas ketidaknyamanan ini dan menghargai kesabaran Anda. Pembaruan lebih lanjut akan segera diberikan.

4. Attention, shoppers! There is a special sale happening at our store this weekend. Enjoy discounts of up to 50% on selected items. Hurry and grab your favorite products before they run out!

Artinya:

Perhatian, para pembelanja! Ada penjualan khusus di toko kami akhir pekan ini. Nikmati diskon hingga 50% untuk barang-barang terpilih. Buruan dan dapatkan produk favorit Anda sebelum kehabisan!

5. Attention, employees! Please remember that the deadline for submitting your monthly reports is tomorrow by 5 PM. Ensure that all necessary documents are complete and submitted on time. Thank you for your cooperation.

Artinya:

Perhatian, karyawan! Mohon diingat bahwa batas waktu pengiriman laporan bulanan Anda adalah besok pukul 5 sore. Pastikan semua dokumen yang diperlukan lengkap dan dikirim tepat waktu. Terima kasih atas kerjasamanya.

22. BERIKAN CONTOH ANNOUNCEMENT ABOUT CORONA DALAM BAHASA INGGRIS


ANNOUNCEMENT TEXT

ANNOUNCEMENT

To: All Students and teachers of SMP 90

Due to the pandemic of coronavirus, here are some things should be noticed:

1. The class will be off starting from tomorrow with the indefinite time. The newest announcement will be announced by the homeroom teachers through online. The homeroom teachers should create the group chat in WhatsApp aplication with the students and students parents.

2. The lesson will be on going through online. The schedule will be arranged by the homeroom teachers. The students must follow the schedule timeline on time. The absence of the online class should be informed by the students an hour at the latest before the lesson started.

3. The students must wear the proper clothes during the online class.

4. Day off does not mean a holiday. The students must stay at home, unless for extremely urgent matters.

5. All students and teachers should wash the hands regularly with soap and wear mask in order to avoid the virus spreading widely. All students and teachers should maintain their health well.

Hopefully, this pandemic will find its final way soon, so we can gather at school in a short time.

Mr. Adams

Head Master

Pembahasan:

Translation:

PENGUMUMAN

Kepada: Semua Siswa dan guru SMP 90

Karena pandemi coronavirus, berikut adalah beberapa hal yang harus diperhatikan:

1. Kelas akan diliburkan mulai besok sampai waktu yang tidak ditentukan. Pengumuman terbaru akan diumumkan oleh guru wali kelas melalui online. Para guru kelas harus membuat obrolan grup dalam aplikasi WhatsApp dengan siswa dan orang tua siswa.

2. Pelajarannya akan berlangsung melalui online. Jadwal akan diatur oleh guru wali kelas. Para siswa harus mengikuti jadwal tepat waktu. Tidak hadir dalam kelas online harus diinformasikan oleh siswa paling lambat satu jam sebelum pelajaran dimulai.

3. Para siswa harus mengenakan pakaian yang sopan selama kelas online.

4. Hari libur bukan berarti liburan. Para siswa harus tinggal di rumah, kecuali untuk hal-hal yang sangat mendesak.

5. Semua siswa dan guru harus mencuci tangan secara teratur dengan sabun dan memakai masker untuk menghindari penyebaran virus secara luas. Semua siswa dan guru harus menjaga kesehatannya dengan baik.

Semoga pandemi ini akan segera berakhir, sehingga kita bisa berkumpul di sekolah dalam waktu singkat.

Bapak Adams

Kepala Sekolah

Teks di atas adalah announcement text tentang hal-hal yang menyangkut kegiatan belajar mengajar selama pandemi terjadi. Dalam announcement text, hal-hal yang disampaikan hanyalah hal-hal yang penting untuk disampaikan dan bahasa yang digunakan jelas, agar tidak menimbulkan pertanyaan baru dari pembaca.

Struktur announcement text:

Judul: ANNOUNCEMENT

Ditujukan kepada: To: All Students and teachers of SMP 90

Pembukaan: Due to the pandemic of coronavirus, here are some things should be noticed:

Isi: 1. The class will be off starting from tomorrow with the indefinite time. The newest announcement will be announced by the homeroom teachers through online. The homeroom teachers should create the group chat in WhatsApp aplication with the students and students parents.

2. The lesson will be on going through online. The schedule will be arranged by the homeroom teachers. The students must follow the schedule timeline on time. The absence of the online class should be informed by the students an hour at the latest before the lesson started.

3. The students must wear the proper clothes during the online class.

4. Day off does not mean a holiday. The students must stay at home, unless for extremely urgent matters.

5. All students and teachers should wash the hands regularly with soap and wear mask in order to avoid the virus spreading widely. All students and teachers should maintain their health well.

Penutup: Hopefully, this pandemic will find its final way soon, so we can gather at school in a short time.

Penulis pengumuman: Mr. Adams

Head Master

Pembahasan

Teks 1

ANNOUNCEMENT


Cancellation of JYJ in Singapore

By Faith & D Entertaiment on Monday. March 28 2011 at 5:19 am


Dear Fans and Media


This is an official announcement to inform everyone that we have just been notified by CJes Entertainment, the artiste agency of JYJ that they have decided to cancel JYJ world tour concert in Singapore which is scheduled on 23 april 2011 at Singapore indoor stadium.

It is with utmost regret that we have to accept this cancellation notice from the artiste agency at this point in time. We have submitted the final plans for stage,seating and ticketing for the agency's approval so as to proceed with the official announcement on ticket sale. These plans are in accordance with the regulations required by authorities in Singapore and the budget allocated for the concert. It is most unfortunate that the plans are not approved by CJes and their decision is to cancel the concert. We respect the agency's decision and,with great regret,we are unable to change their mind but to accept this unforeseen circumstance that is beyond out control.

We understand the disappointment as well as the inconvenience caused due to the cancellation and we sincerely apologize -especially to the fans of JYJ.

The management



Faith & D Entertaiment

39. Berikan contoh struktur announcement dalam bahasa inggris !


Jawaban:

Struktur Umum Announcement

Stating Purpose: menyatakan tujuan dari acara itu diselenggarakan

Stating day and date: menyatakan kapan acara tersebut diselenggarakan

Stating place : menyatakan di mana tempat acara tersebut diselenggarakan

Informing sender ;menyatakan siapa yang melaksanakan acara tersebut dan contact person yang biasa dihubungi.

Contoh Announcement

To: all dorm residents

There will be a blackout each Saturday during this month. Consequently, we will use the generator as the electric source. So, please do not use air conditioners on Saturdays.

Please don’t forget to turn off the lights, if you don’t use it.

Thank You

The Staff Dormitory
