5. buatlah 5 percakapan dalam bahasa inggris situasinya menggunakan To Get Attention​


Berikut ini 5 percakapan yang mengandung ungkapan "To get attention" atau meminta perhatian:

Dialog 1

Ika: Excuse me! Is this your wallet?

Lutfi: Yes, it is.

Ika: You dropped it on the bus station.

Lutfi: Oh my God, thank you so much.

Ika: You are welcome.

Dialog 2

Maryam: Hi, Class! May I have your attention, please?

Class: Sure.

Maryam: Tomorrow is Mrs. Ika's birthday. How if we buy something for her?

Dinda: It's a good idea. Let's buy a veil for her!

Dialog 3

Febri: Hanin!

Hanin: Yes, what's going on?

Febri: Would you help me to bring these books to our principal's room?

Hanin: Of course.

Febri: Thank you.

Hanin: My pleasure.

Dialog 4

Mrs. Ika: Listen up!

Class: Yes, Miss.

Mrs. Ika: I have to go to hospital right now because my son is sick. I want you to study by your self and do exercise on page 45. Do you understand?

Class: Yes, Miss.

Dino: I hope your son get well soon, Miss.

Dialog 5

Gani: Hello, Mr. Anto!

Mr. Anto: Oh hi, Gani! What can I help you?

Gani: I don't really understand about this theory. Would you please explain it?

Mr. Anto: Oh yes, of course.

Pembahasan

Kelima dialog singkat di atas menggunakan kata atau frasa yang digunakan untuk meminta perhatian orang lain. Kat atau frasa yang digunakan juga biasanya diberi tanda seru untuk memberikan penekanan saat mengucapkannya. Berikut ini kata atau frasa yang dapat kita gunakan diantaranya:

Hi/HelloExcuse me!Dino, Siti, Edo (calling name)Listen up! May I have your attention please?
