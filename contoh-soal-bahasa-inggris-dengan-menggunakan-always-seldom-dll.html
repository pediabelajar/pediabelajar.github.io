22. Buatlah kalimat dengan menggunakan bentuk adverb of frequency always,usually, often, sometime,seldom dan Never masing masing 5​


Jawaban:

Simple Present Tense:

Always:

I always watch great movies.You always come to school on time.We always play football together.They always swim in the river.She always drinks fresh milk.

Usually:

I usually go to school on foot.David usually does his homework well.She usually cooks some tasty food.We usually pray in the mosque together.They usually work in the factory on Sundays.

Often:

I often swim alone in an international swimming pool.He often reads new novels.We often hike on the mountain on weekends.They often buy new apartments.She often calls her boyfriend at night.

Sometimes:

I sometimes ride a bicycle.She sometimes cries in her room.We sometimes have dinner in a fancy restaurant.They sometimes win a competition.David and Mark sometimes exchange thoughts in the office.

Seldom:

I seldom drink coffee.He seldom catches a snake.We seldom dive in the ocean.They seldom dig holes in the ground.She seldom shops in a mall.

Never:

I never drink whiskey.She never drinks beer.He never smokes a cigar.We never break our promises.They never fly a jet plane.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Simple Present Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan berulang dengan menggunakan Adverb of Frequency (kata keterangan frekwensi) yaitu Always (selalu), Usually (biasanya), Often (sering), Sometimes (kadang-kadang), Seldom (jarang) dan Never (tidak pernah) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kalimat-kalimatnya:

Always (Selalu):

Saya selalu menonton film bagus. Kamu selalu datang ke sekolah tepat waktu. Kami selalu bermain sepak bola bersama. Mereka selalu berenang di sungai. Dia selalu minum susu segar.

Usually (Biasanya):

Aku biasanya pergi ke sekolah dengan berjalan kaki.David biasanya mengerjakan pekerjaan rumahnya dengan baik.Dia biasanya memasak beberapa makanan enak.Kami biasanya berdoa bersama di masjid.Mereka biasanya bekerja di pabrik pada hari Minggu.

Often (Sering):

Aku sering berenang sendirian di kolam renang internasional. Dia sering membaca novel baru. Kami sering mendaki gunung di akhir pekan. Mereka sering membeli apartemen baru. Dia sering menelepon pacarnya di malam hari.

Sometimes (Kadang-kadang):

Aku terkadang mengendarai sepeda. Dia (cewek) terkadang menangis di kamarnya. Kami terkadang makan malam di restoran mewah. Mereka terkadang memenangkan kompetisi. David dan Mark terkadang bertukar pikiran di kantor.

Seldom (Jarang):

Aku jarang minum kopi. Dia jarang menangkap ular. Kami jarang menyelam di lautan. Mereka jarang menggali lubang di tanah. Dia jarang berbelanja di mal.

Never (Tidak pernah):

Aku tidak pernah minum wiski. Dia tidak pernah minum bir. Dia tidak pernah merokok cerutu. Kami tidak pernah mengingkari janji. Mereka tidak pernah menerbangkan pesawat jet.

Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Adverb of Frequency (Always/Usually/Often/Sometimes/Seldom/Never) + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

32. Jelaskan yang kamu ketahui tentang study abourd seperti perjalanan ,biaya dll menggunakan bahasa Inggris


Jawaban:

Studying abroad refers to the act of pursuing education in a foreign country. This experience offers numerous benefits, such as exposure to diverse cultures, the opportunity to gain a global perspective, and access to high-quality education institutions. Here are some key aspects of studying abroad:

1. **Travel and Exploration**: Studying abroad allows students to explore new countries and experience different cultures. It's a chance to travel, visit historical sites, and immerse oneself in the local way of life.

2. **Academic Opportunities**: Many countries have world-renowned universities and colleges, making it an excellent choice for academic advancement. Students can access a wide range of courses and programs that may not be available in their home country.

3. **Language Acquisition**: Living in a foreign country provides a natural environment to learn or improve language skills. This can be a significant advantage for future career opportunities.

4. **Cultural Exposure**: Studying abroad exposes students to diverse customs, traditions, and perspectives. It fosters cultural awareness and tolerance, which are valuable life skills.

5. **Personal Growth**: Living independently in a foreign country can be a transformative experience. It helps individuals become more self-reliant, adaptable, and open-minded.

6. **Networking**: Studying abroad offers the chance to build an international network of friends and professional contacts, which can be valuable in one's future career.

7. **Costs**: The costs of studying abroad can vary widely depending on the destination and the institution. It's essential to budget for tuition fees, living expenses, travel, and potentially visa fees.

8. **Visa and Immigration**: Students must often obtain a student visa to study abroad. Each country has its immigration requirements and regulations, so it's crucial to research and prepare the necessary documents.

9. **Scholarships and Financial Aid**: Many universities and organizations offer scholarships and financial aid to international students, which can help alleviate some of the costs.

10. **Preparation**: Proper preparation, including researching the destination, securing accommodation, and understanding the local culture, is vital for a successful study abroad experience.

Studying abroad can be a life-changing adventure, providing both educational and personal growth opportunities. However, it requires careful planning and consideration of various factors, including costs, to ensure a rewarding experience.

Next we decided to swim first in the estuary. Because the current is calm unlike in the sea.

PEMBAHASAN:

Simple present tense is used to express general facts / truths, habits (habitual actions), or events that occur at this time (present). Simple present tense is most often used in English because it is used to express daily activities.  

"Simple present tense digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta /kebenaran umum (general truth), kebiasaan (habitual action), atau kejadian yang terjadi pada saat ini (present). Simple present tense paling sering digunakan dalam bahasa Inggris karena digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan sehari-hari"  

Keterangan Waktu Simple Pesent Tense:  

always = selalu  every = tiap  often = sering  usually = biasanya  seldom = jarang  rarely = jarang- jarang  generally = pada umumnya  frequently = seringkali  sometimes = kadang-kadang  ever = pernah  never = tak pernah  

Cara menambahkan akhiran s atau es pada kata kerja:  

Akhiran -es ditambahkan pada kata kerja (verb) yang berhuruf akhir -ch (catches), -s (kisses), -sh (brushes), -x (fixes), -z (whizzes)  Akhiran -es juga ditambahkan pada kata kerja (veb) yang berhuruf akhir -y yang didahului oleh huruf mati dan akan berubah menjadi -i jika mendapat tambahan akhiran -es tersebut. contoh: study=studies, carry=carries, try=tries.  Sedangkan bila -y didahului oleh huruf hidup, maka kerja itu hanya akan mendapat akhiran -s. contoh :pay-pays, say=says  Akhiran -es juga ditambahkan khusus bagi kata kerja do dan go. contoh: do=does, go=goes  

Formula:  

Verbal sentences: kalimat yang predikatnya adalah kata kerja  

(+) Subject + Verb1 s/es  (-) Subject + do/does + not + Verb1  (?) Do/does + Subject + Verb1?  

Nominal sentence: kalimat yang predikatnya adalah bukan kata kerja  

(+) Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Object/Complement  (-) Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Object/Complement  (?) To be (is/am/are) +Subject + Object/Complement?  

Perhatikan cara penggunaan kata kerja bantu (auxiliary verb) pada kalimat simple present tense berikut ini!

Kata kerja bantu (auxiliary verb) "do" digunakan untuk subject I, you, we dan they atau kata nama diri dalam bentuk jamak.  Kata kerja bantu (auxiliary verb) "does" digunakan untuk subject he, she dan it atau kata nama diri dalam bentuk tunggal.  

Perhatikan penjelasan bagaimana menggunakan to be di dalam kalimat nominal berikut ini!  

Tobe "is" digunakan untuk subject he, she dan it atau kata nama diri dalam bentuk tunggal.  Tobe "am" diguakan untuk subject I  Tobe "are" digunakan untuk subject you, we dan they atau kata nama diri dalam bentuk jamak.
