21. mencari teks b Inggris expresing dan asking for​


Berikut kalimat yang mengandung expressing asking and giving opinion:

Asking for opinion: "Do you think it will include on essay?"Giving opinion: "I think it will cover chapter 5 in our book".

Pembahasan

Pada soal kita diminta untuk mencari kalimat yang mengandung expression asking and giving opinion. Asking for opinion ialah menanyakan pendapat, sedangkan giving opinion ialah memberikan pendapat. Berikut terjemahan dari kalimat di atas:

Meminta pendapat: "Apakah menurut Anda itu akan dimasukkan ke dalam esai?" Memberikan pendapat: "Saya pikir itu akan mencakup bab 5 dalam buku kami".

25. contoh percakapan bahasa inggris expresing certainty, expressing admiration, asking for repetition

Elly : really(asking for certainty)? Jack : I'm quite sure(responding for certainty), it's so delicious. Would you share the recipe for me? Elly : first, prepare the ingredients such as : leftover rice, unions, garlic, soy sauce, cooking oil, eggs, corn, carrot, cabbage. Jack : how to make it? Elly : fried the unions and garlic until yellowish. Then, beat the eggs and fry the eggs. Jack : sorry, I can't hear you(asking repetition) Elly : I said(responding repetition) fried the unions and garlic until yellowish. Then, beat the eggs and fry the eggs. Jack : tell me more. Elly : then add the corn, carrot and cabbage. After that add the rice and soy sauce. Finally, serve it. Jack : oh.. it's so easy. I will try to make it. Thank you Elly, see you.
