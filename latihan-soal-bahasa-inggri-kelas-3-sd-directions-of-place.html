6. make a dialogue talking about locations and directions!Soal kelas 6 SDTolong dijawab ​


Jawaban:

A: Hi (B)! Where are you going?

B: Hi (A)! I'm trying to find the public library, but I'm lost.

A: Oh, let me help you with that. You are really close already. You just need to head straight and turn right at the end of this street. The library is between the police station and the school around that corner. You can't miss it.

B: Oh, thank you very much (A)!

A: You're welcome!

Penjelasan:

A: Halo (B)! Kamu mau kemana?

B: Halo (A)! Aku mau ke perpustakaan umum, tapi aku tersesat.

A: Oh, mari saya bantu dengan itu. Kamu sudah dekat. Kmau tinggal jalan lurus terus dan belok kanan di ujung jalan ini. Perpustakaannya ada di antara kantor polisi dan sekolah. Kamu tidak akan melewatkannya.

B: Oh, terima kasih banyak (A)!

A: Sama-sama!

(FYI A dan B silahkan diganti dengan nama orang kalau mau)

32. Contoh soal bahasa inggris desceription of place


My favorite place which is important to me is my countryside house. I have a very bright impressions and memories related with it. That is the place where I have grown up, met my friends and where I have spent almost all my life.

It is a big house with a yard. In summer there are a lot of flowers which make the outlook beautiful. In the backyard there are place, where I love to spend time, lying in hammock and dreaming under the tree.

Inside the house there are kitchen, big living room, where you can invite guests, friends and chat with them, watch TV and relax. Next to the living room there are rooms, which are are always sunlit during daytime, so you wake up with a very good mood in the morning.

The house has a very homely atmosphere, I will always like this place, because I have many good, pleasant memories and feelings about it.
