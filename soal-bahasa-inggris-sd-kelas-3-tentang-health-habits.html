9. habits dragonfly dan damselfly​


Dragonfly

Dragonflies are classified as carnivores. They usually eat mosquitoes, moths, ants, termites, bees, butterflies, and flies.

Dragonflies also eat larvae, tadpoles and small fish. The nymph nymph (the first stage after hatching) likes to eat mosquitoes.

Adult dragonflies can eat around 50 mosquitoes in a day while flying so that they act as biological mosquito controllers..

A dragonfly is an insect belonging to the 'Odonata' sequence. Capunga is not really flying even though they both have six legs and three parts of the body, head, chest and abdomen. The main difference between them is that flies only have two wings while dragonflies have four wings. Dragonflies are sometimes confused with Damselflies. Even though they are both members of the same order, they have little difference where when resting, damselflies hold their wings together, dragonflies hold their wings horizontally or slightly down and forward and their hindwings are wider near the base. The eyes in a damselfly are separated, in part the dragonfly touches. However, being in the same position makes their life cycle very similar. The names of dragonflies come from their fierce jaws, which they use to catch their prey.

11. buatlah cerita tentang hewan menggunakan bahasa Inggris dan buatlah tabel species habitat habits physical characteristics emotional characteristics and functions tentang hewan itutolong jawab ​


Once upon a time, in a dense forest lived a family of orangutans. The family consisted of a mother, father, and a baby orangutan. They were one of the most intelligent and fascinating animals living in the forest.

Species: Orangutan

Habitat Dense Forests

Habits: Arboreal and Diurnal

Physical Characteristics: Covered with shaggy reddish-brown hair, long arms, broad chest, and strong jaws

Emotional Characteristics: Playful, affectionate, and curious

Functions: Seed dispersal, maintain forest ecosystem, attraction for ecotourism


The orangutans lived high up in the trees, and they were known for their amazing climbing skills. They would spend most of their day in search of food, swinging from tree to tree, and picking fruits and leaves.

Orangutans are covered with shaggy reddish-brown hair, which keeps them warm in the cool forest environment. Their long arms and broad chest help them move effortlessly through the trees, and their strong jaws allow them to crack open hard nuts and seeds.

Apart from their physical characteristics, orangutans are known for their playful, affectionate, and curious nature. They are highly social animals, and they love spending time with their family members. The baby orangutan would often playfully swing from tree to tree, under the watchful eye of its parents.

Orangutans play a crucial role in the forest ecosystem. They are responsible for dispersing seeds of various fruits and trees, which helps to maintain the balance of the forest. They are also a major attraction for ecotourism, which helps to generate revenue for the local communities.

In conclusion, orangutans are fascinating animals, known for their intelligence, climbing skills, and affectionate nature. They play a crucial role in maintaining the forest ecosystem and are an important attraction for ecotourism.

Person A: Exactly! Like any other food, moderation is key. If you enjoy instant noodles occasionally and pair them with nutritious ingredients, they can be a part of a balanced diet.

23. sebutkan 15 mental health contohtraumatic= traumadepprecssion = depresi(di kamus b Inggris)bantu jawab yh soal besok aku mau ujian ​


Tentu, berikut adalah 15 istilah masalah kesehatan mental dan deskripsi singkatnya:

1. Anxiety (Kecemasan): Ketidaknyamanan yang berlebihan dan kekhawatiran yang mungkin tidak proporsional terhadap situasi.

2. Depression (Depresi): Perasaan yang dalam dan berkelanjutan dari sedih, kehilangan minat, dan kehilangan energi.

3. Bipolar Disorder (Gangguan Bipolar): Gangguan mental dengan perubahan suasana hati yang drastis antara mania (tingkat energi yang tinggi) dan depresi.

4. Schizophrenia (Skizofrenia): Gangguan mental serius yang mempengaruhi pikiran, perasaan, dan perilaku, seringkali dengan gejala seperti delusi dan halusinasi.

5. Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (Gangguan Obsesif-Kompulsif): Mengalami pikiran obsesif yang tak terkendali dan perilaku kompulsif yang berulang.

6. Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (Gangguan Stres Pasca Trauma): Gejala yang berkembang setelah pengalaman traumatis, termasuk mimpi buruk, kecemasan, dan ketergangguan tidur.

7. Eating Disorders (Gangguan Makan): Gangguan pola makan yang mencakup anoreksia (menghindari makan) dan bulimia (muntah disengaja setelah makan).

8. Borderline Personality Disorder (Gangguan Kepribadian Ambang): Gangguan mental yang ditandai dengan perubahan cepat dalam suasana hati, citra diri yang tidak stabil, dan hubungan antarpribadi yang bergejolak.

9. Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (Gangguan Pemusatan Perhatian/Hiperaktivitas): Kesulitan dalam memusatkan perhatian, impulsivitas, dan hiperaktivitas yang mengganggu aktivitas sehari-hari.

10. Social Anxiety Disorder (Gangguan Kecemasan Sosial): Rasa takut yang kuat terhadap situasi sosial atau pertemuan dengan orang lain.

11. Substance Use Disorder (Gangguan Penggunaan Zat): Ketergantungan atau penyalahgunaan zat seperti narkotika atau alkohol.

12. Panic Disorder (Gangguan Panik): Serangan panik tiba-tiba yang ditandai dengan gejala fisik dan emosional yang intens.

13. Seasonal Affective Disorder (Gangguan Afektif Musiman): Depresi yang terjadi pada musim tertentu, biasanya saat musim dingin dengan kurangnya cahaya matahari.

14. Insomnia (Susah Tidur): Kesulitan tidur atau tidur yang tidak nyenyak secara teratur.

15. Trauma-Related Disorders (Gangguan Terkait Trauma): Meliputi gangguan terkait trauma lainnya seperti gangguan stres akut dan gangguan adaptasi dengan unsur stres.

Ingatlah bahwa ini hanyalah istilah umum dan masing-masing masalah kesehatan mental memiliki nuansa dan kompleksitas tersendiri.
