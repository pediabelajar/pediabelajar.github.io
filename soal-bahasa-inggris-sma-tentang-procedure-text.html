PERSAMAAN (equality/sameness)

1. General Purpose (tujuan umum): Both procedure text and manual procedure have the general purpose of providing instructions or guidance on how to perform a specific action or process.

2. Similar Structure (struktur serupa): They both have a similar structure, consisting of steps or a sequence of actions that must be followed by the reader or user.

DIFFERENCE (perbedaan)

1. Content and Expertise (konten dan keahlian): Procedure Text is for everyday tasks like cooking or personal care and is easy to understand. Manual Procedure is for technical or professional tasks, often requiring specialized knowledge.

2. Context (konteks): Procedure Text is used in everyday situations, while Manual Procedure is used in technical or industrial settings where precision and safety are critical.
