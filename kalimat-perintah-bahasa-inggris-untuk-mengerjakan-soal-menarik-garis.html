13. kalimat perintah dalam bahasa Inggris​


1. Say it right now!

(Katakan sekarang!)

2. Don’t step on the grass!

(Jangan menginjak rumput!)

3. Be quick! I don’t want to be late.

(Cepatlah! Saya tidak ingin terlambat)

4. Don’t be late! The meeting will not start without you.

(Jangan terlambat! Pertemuan itu tidak akan dimulai tanpa kamu.)

5. Submit the report to the manager right now!

(Kumpulkan laporan itu kepada manajer sekarang juga!)

6. Submit your homework by tomorrow!

(Kumpulkan pekerjaan rumah (PR) kalian besok!)

7. Jump to the boat right now!

(Lompat ke perahu itu sekarang!)

8. Get out from my house! I do not want to see you anymore.

(Keluar dari rumahku sekarang! Saya tidak ingin melihatmu lagi.)

9. Don’t turn off the light! Your brother is scared of dark.

(Jangan tutup lampunya! Adikmu takut akan kegelapan.)

10. Just shut up! I do not want to hear anything from you.

(Tutup saja mulutmu! Saya tidak ingin mendengar apapun darimu.)

22. Tolong bantu saya untuk mengerjakan soal bahasa Inggris ini


Reading Expository Text: Instruction/Procedure Text

Expository text adalah text yang menjelaskan, menginformasikan dan meng-edukasi pembacanya.

Beberapa contoh dari expository text adalah artikel berita, instruksi manual, text books, ensiklopedia dan journal.

PembahasanThe purpose of the text above is to inform us about how to use a new phone for the first time before making a phone call.What should we do after inserting the SIM card? We should insert the battery inside."These are the ways..." The synonym of the underlined word is method or technique.What does the text tell us about? The text tells us about the step-by-step in installing a new printer on our Windows laptop.What is the first operation we should take in installing our office printer on a windows laptop? The first operation is to get an IP address.The phrase of "a couple of copies" What does the sentence mean? It means that there are a few of CDs with the driver for the printer in the office. A couple in here doesn't always mean two, but it can be a few or several, mean more than two.What is the procedure about? The procedure about doing online check-in.What should we do before we enter the boarding gate? We should complete the check-in process, print the online boarding pass for each passenger and bring this with you."....you will be issued an online boarding pass..." The underlined word can be replaced by supply or provide.Make a procedure text: Topic How to buy an online-purchaseFirst, you have to decide what product you want to buy, for example you want to buy a new laptop.Go to the search engine and google for "new laptop" or if you already know the shop website you can directly go to that website. Make sure that the website you choose is a trusted site.Search the product by typing the keyword product you already have in your mind. Read the descriptions of the product. If this is what you're looking for, put them in the basket.Proceed to check out and you can pay your items by credit card/Pay Pal, choose the method of payment you want. Choose the delivery system for example : shipping, delivery door to door or pick up.Insert your details : card number, your name, expiry date of the cardAfter entering your details, click pay. And wait for the confirmation.A confirmation will be sent to your email address.Pelajari Lebih Lanjut
