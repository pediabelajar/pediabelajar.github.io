Penjelasan:

1. Awal pertanyaan menggunakan "Do", pertanyaan menuju kepada "you" yang dimana jawaban yang tepat adalah menggunakan "I".

2. Awal pertanyaan menggunakan "Do", pertanyaan menuju kepada "you" yang dimana jawaban yang tepat adalah menggunakan "I".

3. Awal pertanyaan menggunakan "Does", pertanyaan menuju kepada ayah, ayah berkelamin pria "he".

4. Awal pertanyaan menggunakan "Does", pertanyaan menuju kepada hujan, kata ganti hujan yang menggunakan "does" adalah he/she/it, karena hujan ialah suatu benda, maka yang digunakan adalah "it".

5. Awal pertanyaan menggunakan "Do", pertanyaan menuju kepada "you" yang dimana jawaban yang tepat adalah menggunakan "I".

6. Awal pertanyaan menggunakan "Does", pertanyaan menuju kepada ibu, ibu berkelamin wanita "she".

7. Awal pertanyaan menggunakan "Do", pertanyaan menuju kepada "you" yang dimana jawaban yang tepat adalah menggunakan "I".

8. Awal pertanyaan menggunakan "Do", pertanyaan menuju kepada "you" yang dimana jawaban yang tepat adalah menggunakan "I".

9. Awal pertanyaan menggunakan "Do", pertanyaan menuju kepada "babies" yang dimana kata pengganti babies adalah "they".

10. Awal pertanyaan menggunakan "Do", pertanyaan menuju kepada "cats" yang dimana kata pengganti cats adalah "they".

7. Bantuin buat daily activities makek bahasa inggris yg panjang dong


From Monday to Saturday, I usually wake up at 5 o’clock in the morning. I need to prepare myself to school. There are many activities I have to do before going to school.

First of all, I do my bed until it is very tidy. After that, I sweep the floor. I love to see my room neat and clean. When I still feel sleepy, I walk around my house. Also, I do some physical movements to stretch my body.

Then, I prepare some books based on schedule. I do not want to leave any book at home. I wash my face, brush my teeth, then have a shower. Next, I eat breakfast after wearing my school uniform. Before going to school I hug my parents. I'm going to school by bycycle because it's eco friendly.

At 6.30 a.m I arrived at school and go to my class. The bell ring at 7 p.m so I begin to learning. Then, when rest time I like to chat with my friends and eat some food at the canteen. Next, at 10 a.m, rest time is over and I return to the class to continue study.

The school finishes at 1 p.m. After school, I change my uniform then have lunch. Then, I go to play at my friend house. At 4 p.m I go back home and have a shower.

Dinner time, I usually help my parents set the table then help to wash dishes after eating. Then I watching TV for one hour. After that, I study and do my homework. At the end, I go to sleep at 9 p.m.

Semoga membantu..

28. makalah bahasa inggris tentang family


My Lovely Family

For me, family is something that is very precious because nothing can replace the role of the family in this world. That’s a place where I could find a peaceful and everlasting affection. I really love my family. My family consists of four people they are my father, mother and sister. Although we are just a small family, our affection to each other is very large. I’m very proud of them. And now I’ll tell you about my family.

My father is a disciplined person. He always teaches me and my sister not to waste our time. My father is so different with other disciplined person. Even though he is so discipline, he remains friendly and understands our needs. My father is a lawyer who is quite famous for its decisiveness. He always decides a case fairly and impartially person or group. My father is almost always busy with his work but he never forgets his family. He always invites us to go to vacation every weekend. He thinks our happiness is his happiness too.My mom is just a housewife. Actually he used to be a teacher. However, my father asked her to stop and focus on taking care of the family. She is a woman who is very patient and diligent in taking care of the family. She always does her task with full of love. I think my mother is also the best chef. She always cooks delicious food for us. However, sometimes she is very fussy but I realizes that it was all for our own good.

My sister named Putri. She is a sister who is very spoiled. Sometimes she is annoying and often makes me angry. Even though, I am very fond of her. She is still Study at Senior High School grade 3rd. She has a dream to become a lawyer like my father. My father is very happy to know my sister’s dream. He always supports my sister to make it comes true.

Semoga Membantu :)
