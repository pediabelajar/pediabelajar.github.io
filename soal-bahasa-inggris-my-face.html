How has the pandemic made me think about education differently? menurut pendapat kamu2. How do I face my fears ? menurut pendapat kamu


Jawaban:

First of all, during this pandemic, we all know that we should not do any kinds of gathering activities. That includes going to school. The fact that you couldn't meet your teachers and friends in person, already affects our way of studying from home. Even though we could still use some sort of studying application, such as Zoom, Google Classroom, etc. It's still unsure if every student could understand their lessons without the need of asking the teacher in person. That's why I think that pandemic negatively affects our education system. But there's still a positive effect of this pandemic on our education system. Some schools that aren't used to technology, are now forced to innovate and are more open to such studying applications. Thus making the education system adapt to the latest information and tech.What makes a pandemic more scary than it actually is, are the hysteria reacting to it. Of course you shouldn't underestimate the virus, but you shouldn't overreact too. One of the ways to overcome this fear is to learn more about the virus, and ways to prevent the virus from spreading. The more you understand it. The more you think rationally and take the right steps.
