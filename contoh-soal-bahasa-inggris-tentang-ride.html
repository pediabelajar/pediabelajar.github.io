2. contih dialog bahasa inggris tentang not ride motorbike to school


On the road to school, the question of riding is really no different than any other destination, taking into account road and traffic conditions as well as your own ability to ride safely while carrying books or other school materials.

The question of whether you should ride to school might have more to do with the degree of assurance you have that your motorcycle will not be bothered by your fellow students. If your school requires parking permits and/or has designated motorcycle parking then these are things you should know ahead of time before simply showing up at a school which lacks any provision to properly park your vehicle.

I began riding my motorcycle to school way back in 8th grade, along with my brother and a neighborhood friend, as we all had small-bore motorcycles at the time and lived in a part of town that didn’t have bus service. We started off by chaining our motorcycles to the bicycle rack since there was nowhere else to park them, but this left less room for the students who brought bicycles. We were all called into the principal’s office one day and told we couldn’t bring the cycles anymore. We explained that we had no bus service in our neighborhood and our working parents did not have the time to take us to school every morning. The principal surprisingly changed her mind, and set aside one of the teacher’s car parking spots for our use. So not only did we get to drive to school in our pre-teen years, but we were even given private parking. It made us all feel like some big shots.

4. Contoh Soal Ujian Sekolah SD my - will - bike - I - ride - Sudirman Street - to. The correct sentence is ... A. I will my bike ride to Sudirman Street B. To Sudirman Street I will ride my bike C. I will ride my bike to Sudirman Street D. My bike to Sudirman Street I will ride


The correct sentence is I will ride my bike to Sudirman Street (C). The text above uses Simple Future Tense, which states activities that will occur in the future.

Pembahasan

Teks di atas menggunakan Simple Future Tense, atau tenses yang menyatakan aktivitas yang akan terjadi di masa depan. Hal ini ditandai dengan kata yang digunakan pada kalimat yang belum tersusun di atas, yaitu kata "will", dan "bike" yang berupa kata kerja bentuk pertama yang merupakan salah satu ciri-ciri Simple Future Tense.

Rumus dari Simple Future itu sendiri adalah sebagai berikut:

Subject + Will + Verb 1 + O/C

Berdasarkan rumus tersebut, kita dapat mengetahui bahwa kata pertama yang tepat adalah "I" sebagai subjek, disusul kata "will" sehingga pilihan jawaban B dan C kurang tepat. Selanjutnya terdapat penggunaan Verb 1, di mana ada kata "ride" dalam kalimat tersebut sebagai kata kerja. Terakhir, terdapat objek (my bike) dan pelengkap (to Sudirman Street) yang berupa kata keterangan tempat (Adverb of Place). Oleh karena itu, pilihan jawaban yang tepat adalah C.

Kata ‘a’, ‘an’, dan ‘the’ adalah jenis kata artikel yang terbagi menjadi dua kategori, yaitu definite dan indefinite. Kata ‘the’ termasuk definite, yang berarti sudah pasti. Sedangkan kata ‘a’ dan ‘an’ termasuk indefinite, yang berarti tidak pasti atau secara general. Kita menggunakan kata ‘the’ saat kita ingin menyatakan suatu hal yang pasti. Kata ‘a’ dan ‘an’ digunakan saat kita ingin menyatakan suatu hal yang sifatnya umum atau general.

"a" digunakan sebelum kata benda tunggal yang dimulai dengan konsonan. Contohnya : a boy, a bike, a car.

"the” digunakan untuk mengacu pada kata benda yang sifatnya spesifik atau khusus. “The” bisa digunakan sebelum kata benda tunggal dan jamak, serta kata benda tak terhitung.

39. dialog bahasa inggris tentang A plan to ride on a bicycle to exercise on the weekends


Accepting/Declining Invitation

Nana: Bella, would you like to join us on the weekend? We’re going to ride on our bicycle to the city park.

Marsya: That’s true! It would be so much fun if you could come and join us.
