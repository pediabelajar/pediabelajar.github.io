18. Bossy = Controlling, Tyrannical (ADJECTIVE): Suka memerintah

19. Baffle = confuse, deceive (ADJECTIVE): membingunkan

20. Hypocrisy = falseness (NOUN): Kemunafikan

Penjelasan:

Yang pertama ada kata Sinonim, Sinonim adalah persamaan kata dengan arti sama atau hampir. Maksudnya, kata yang bersinonim bisa jadi berbeda tetapi memiliki makna yang serupa. Dalam sudut pandang bahasa, sinonim membuat sebuah kalimat menjadi lebih menarik.

Kalimat yang menggunakan sinonim adalah lebih enak diungkapkan. Itu artinya tidak banyak kata serupa yang diungkap berulang-ulang. Hal ini juga sudah pasti membuat pembaca dan pendengar lebih kaya pengetahuan.

Lalu ada Kata Antonim, Menurut KBBI, antonim adalah kata yang maknanya berlawanan dari kata lain. Antonim selalu mengacu pada pembenturan makna yang tidak akan pernah bersahabat atau selalu berlawanan.

•To showcase the national batik day, our school held a fashion show of batik-patterned kebaya from traditional to modern styles.

•In the museum there are various styles of houses, both traditional to modern, we can see here.

3. Original >< Fake

•In one of the television stations, I once followed the original or fake program.

•To distinguish real money and counterfeit money, my mother bought a money detector.

4. New >< Old

•To welcome Eid, my mother bought new curtains to replace the old curtains.

•I hardly recognize the road to his house anymore, because the old resident's house is now changing to a new one.

5. Happy >< Sad

•We want to live this life happily and sadly, only ourselves who determine it.

•We should live, we should always be grateful to the Creator, both when our lives are happy or sad.

Sinonim :

1. Reality = fact

•Sometimes expectations do not match reality so we must have a patient and sincere soul.

•I hope that after so many years I have known you, there will be no lies between us.

•When we commit a falsehood, it will be followed by another falsehood.

3. Primary = main

•Persib is a Bandung city football team that has won the premier league title for the first time.

•The most important thing in achieving your goals is to study hard and don't forget to pray.

4. Appearance = face

•When I saw the woman's Appearance, I thought of my mother at home.

•Face is indeed a parameter of a person's beauty.

5. Undoubtedly = Definitely

•If you study seriously, undoubtedly all tomorrow's exams will be passed well.

•If you can achieve a score above the average, then you will definitely pass the selection tomorrow.
