*kevin walks to his friends*

Kevin : Hello friends! How are you today?

Azhar : *surprised* oh! Kevin! you made me shock.

Kevin : oh i'm very sorry...

Azhar : no it's ok... i'm fine thank you.

Kevin : How about you Andre, and Satria?

Andre : i'm not very well. But, at least i still can go to school.

Satria : i'm fine...how about yourself, Kevin?

Kevin : as you see, i'm really happy!

Satria : You look so excited, why?

Kevin : You know...today is a beautiful day after the long rainy season...i feel  much better.

Andre : i agree...it's a beautiful day.

Azhar : yeah, but we almost late for school...we better run before we get detention!

All : oh yeah! let's gooo!!! * run to class*
