14. Sebutkan contoh soal business letter dan jawabannya


Jawaban:

Business letters adalah jenis surat yang biasanya digunakan dalam perihal perniagaan atau administrasi perkantoran. Ada beberapa jenis business letters yang termasuk sering digunakan seperti letter of inquiry, letter of complaint, letter of apology, letter of order and request dan lainnya.

Berikut ini adalah salah satu contoh business letter. (Letter of inquiry)

Dear Mr.Villalibre,

I am writing to enquire in relation to one of your instagram account which offers the football boots product, 'Casa de Villalibre'. I am interested in conducting discussion on the collaboration with my company which runs the football academy for the young adult. I wonder if you could inform me the details of your product mentioned in your social media and schedule an online meeting this week.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Best Regards,

Ahmed Al-Hilal

Penjelasan:

Berikut contoh soalnya:

1. What does the sender want to ask to Mr.Villalibre? The permission to discuss about the collaboration project and the details of the product.

2. Which platform does Mr.Al-Hilal know about Mr.Villalibre's product?

He knows it from Instagram.

3. When is the suggested online meeting expected to be held? This week.

4. What is the meaning of 'conduct'?

It means to 'hold' or 'do'.

25. soal personal letter beserta jawabannya


Q : what is the structure of personal letter?
A  : 1. Date : Date when the letter is writen (top left).
      2. Address : Place where you are writing fron (top right).
      3. Salutation & Name : Greeting and the person’s name you are writing to.
      4. Introduction : The opening of the letter usually starts with “how are you?”  or refers to previous letter.
      5. Body : The main part of the letter. It includes what you want to write to the other person.
      6. Closure : The part indicates the letter is going to end.
      7. Complimentary close : Short expressions like “love you” , “sincerely yours” , “love”.
      8. Signature : Signature or initials of the writer.
      9. Postscript P.S : After thougt in a letter. You begin with P.S. and end it with your initials.
