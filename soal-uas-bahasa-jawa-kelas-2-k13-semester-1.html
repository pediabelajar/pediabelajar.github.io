Text 1 Early one morning, Umar was walking to the bus stop to catch a bus to school. A loud, screeching noise suddenly pierced the air. Umar realized that there had been an accident. A small boy was lying unconscious on the road, and the car that had hit him had already run away. Umar was confused and frightened. “is the boy dead?” Umar asked himself. However, the boy was still breathing. Umar could see that the boy had bruises all over his face and arms. Umar did not know what to do. Few cars passed along this road at this time of the day. The nearest house which he could go to for help was his own, but it would take ten minutes to walk there. Umar finally decided to stay with the boy until somebody came by. Time passed very slowly – so it seemed to Umar. The boy was still unconscious, and Umar prayed for the boy to regain consciousness. Umar sprang to his feet at the sound of an approaching car. He waved frantically at the driver to stop. Umar asked the driver,”You will help this boy, won,t you, Sir?” The driver was kind. He carried the injuried boy to the car. Umar went with them to the hospital. Later, the driver took Umar to the police station to report the accident. Umar had a lot to tell to his friends that day at school. All his friends though Umar was a very brave boy.”it was an unforgettable incident, wasn,t it?” They asked him. “Yes, it was, “Umar replied.”it was a terrifying experience, thought!”

1. Negara berikut yang sangat sering dilanda angin topan adalah ....
a. Australia
b. Singapura
c. Malaysia
d. Brunai Darussalam

2. Salah satu usaha untuk mencegah terjadinya banjir adalah ....
a. Rehabilitasi
b. Irigasi
c. Reboisasi
d. Urbanisasi

3. Keadaan cuaca di Indonesia selalu di pantau oleh badan yang disebut ....
a. KPK
b. Bakornas
c. LSM
d. BMKG

4. Gejala alam yang sering terjadi di Filipina adalah ....
a. Gunung meletus dan badai tropis
b. Badai salju dan longsor
c. Banjir dan erosi
d. Gempa bumi dan tsunami

5. Membuang sampah di sungai dapat menyebabkan bencana ....
a. Banjir
b. Gempa
c. Longsor
d. Kebakaran

6. Bila ada tanda-tanda akan terjadinya tsunami, yang segera kita lakukan adalah ....
a. Mendekati pantai untuk mengukur besarnya gelombang
b. Pergi menjauhi pantai dan mencari tempat yang tinggi
c. Tinggal di dalam rumah
d. Sembunyi di bawah tempat tidur

7. Arti dari globalisasi adalah ....
a. Adanya ketergantungan satu negara dengan negara lain
b. Proses hilangnya identitas suatu bangsa
c. Proses mendunia dalam segala aspek kehidupan
d. Proses hancurnya budaya lokal oleh budaya dunia

8. Apabila terjadi gempa dan berada di dalam rumah, hal yang harus dilakukan adalah ....
a. Berlindung di bawah meja
b. Lari ke kamar
c. Menyelamatkan harta benda
d. Lari ke luar

9. Secara geografis Indonesia terletak di antara dua benua yaitu ....
a. Asia dan Eropa
b. Eropa dan Afrika
c. Asia dan Afrika
d. Asia dan Australia

10. Alat untuk mencatat gempa bumi disebut ....
a. Seismograf
b. Anemometer
c. Seismologi
d. Termometer

11. Hal yang harus dilakukan jika di suatu tempat mengalami bencana alam adalah ....
a. Melihatnya
b. Memberi bantuan
c. Membahasnya
d. Tak  peduli

12. Berikut ini penyebab terjadinya globalisasi adalah kemajuan dalam bidang....
a. Politik ekonomi
b. Persenjataan
c. Sosial budaya
d. Ilmu Pengetahuan dan teknologi

13. Negara-negara di kawasan Asia Tenggara hampir seluruhnya beriklim....
a. Subtropis
b. Kontinental
c. Tropis
d. Gurun

14. Di Indonesia, badai yang sering terjadi dan banyak menyebabkan korban adalah badai ....
a. Tornado
b. Laut
c. Salju
d. Tropis

15. Kawasan yang rawan terjadi tanah longsor adalah ....
a. Lereng perbukitan
b. Rawa-rawa
c. Dataran rendah
d. Pantai
