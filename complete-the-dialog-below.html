35. activity 1 complete the dialog below with the correct expression from the box!


Answer:

In completing the dialogue, you need to think of the missing part of speech or possible words based on the words or phrases before and after the gaps as well as from the option available. Here are the answers:
1. Why did you...
2. Your class ....
3. (where are you going?) is better than 'where will you go? ' as the teacher is certainly planning to go home so it's a fixed plan not simply a spontaneous decision.
4. Terrible Stomachache
5. Better soon
6. Attention please
7. Have to do
8. Your voice
9. Your attention ( it's better to say 'may I have your attention' instead of 'please give me your attention' as it sounds weird in English context.)
10. Our teacher
