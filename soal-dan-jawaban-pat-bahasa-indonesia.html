23. berikan jawaban latihan soal pat /ukk kelas 8 Bahasa Inggris beri kunci jawaban nya​


Soal 1:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

My sister ________ tennis every Saturday morning.

a) plays

b) play

c) played

d) playing

Jawaban: a) plays

Soal 2:

Complete the sentence with the correct preposition:

I'm going to the store ________ some groceries.

a) at

b) with

c) for

d) to

Jawaban: c) for

Soal 3:

Choose the correct question tag for the following sentence:

He doesn't like coffee, ________?

a) does he?

b) isn't he?

c) doesn't he?

d) don't he?

Jawaban: a) does he?

Soal 4:

Identify the correct comparative form of the adjective "good":

a) gooder

b) goodest

c) better

d) goodier

Jawaban: c) better

Soal 5:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

She ________ a book when I arrived.

a) reads

b) is reading

c) read

d) has read

Jawaban: b) is reading

Soal 6:

Choose the correct word order for the sentence:

carrots / do / like / you / ?

a) You do like carrots?

b) Do you like carrots?

c) Carrots you do like?

d) Do like you carrots?

Jawaban: b) Do you like carrots?

Soal 7:

Identify the correct spelling of the word:

a) beutiful

b) beutifull

c) beautiful

d) beautifull

Jawaban: c) beautiful

Soal 8:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

The students ________ to the teacher's instructions.

a) listens

b) listen

c) listened

d) listening

Jawaban: b) listen

Soal 9:

Choose the correct possessive form of the noun:

The dog is ________ bone.

a) its

b) it's

c) their

d) they're

Jawaban: a) its

Soal 10:

Identify the correct punctuation for the sentence:

My favorite color is blue ________.

a) .

b) ,

c) !

d) ?

Jawaban: a) .

33. Tolong jawab ya soalnya buat besok (Yang dicoret biru) .soal PAT​


Jawab:

B. 1408 cm²

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Diketahui:

diameter = 14 cm

jari-jari (r) = 1/2 diameter = 1/2 x 14 = 7 cm

tinggi (t) = 25 cm

Luas permukaan tabung :

= 2 π r ( r + t )

= 2 x 22/7 x 7 x (7 + 25)

= 44 x 32

= 1408 cm²

15. B. 15 ekor

18. D. 325 siswa

19. C. 28

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

15. Rata-rata (mean)

= jumlah data

banyak data

= 10 + 12 + 9 + 15 + 18 + 20 + 21

7

= 105 = 15 (B)

7

16. Rata rata nilai Rinto = 5,8 dari 4 kali ulangan

Total nilai Rinto = 5,8 × 4 = 23,2

Agar Rinto meraih rata rata nilai ulangan 6,0

Maka Rinto harus meraih total nilai 24

24 – total nilai Rinto = 24 – 23,2 = 0,8

Rinto minimal harus mendapat nilai

5,8 + 0,8 = 6,6 (B)

18. Rata-rata (mean)

= jumlah data

banyak data

= 350 + 395 + 275 + 380 + 300 + 250

6

= 1.950 = 325 (D)

6

19. Jika ada 55 data, maka median data tersebut terletak pada data ke 28

55 ÷ 2 = 27,5 jadi median nya ada pada ada ke 28

20. Nilai rata-rata = 6,67 dari 6 kali ulangan

Nilai = 8, n, 7, 6, 7, 5

Jumlah nilai = 6,67 × 6 = 40

N = 40 – 8 – 7 – 6 – 7 – 5 = 7 (C)
