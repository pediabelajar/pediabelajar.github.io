4. berikan jawaban latihan soal pat /ukk kelas 8 Bahasa Inggris beri kunci jawaban nya​


Soal 1:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

My sister ________ tennis every Saturday morning.

a) plays

b) play

c) played

d) playing

Jawaban: a) plays

Soal 2:

Complete the sentence with the correct preposition:

I'm going to the store ________ some groceries.

a) at

b) with

c) for

d) to

Jawaban: c) for

Soal 3:

Choose the correct question tag for the following sentence:

He doesn't like coffee, ________?

a) does he?

b) isn't he?

c) doesn't he?

d) don't he?

Jawaban: a) does he?

Soal 4:

Identify the correct comparative form of the adjective "good":

a) gooder

b) goodest

c) better

d) goodier

Jawaban: c) better

Soal 5:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

She ________ a book when I arrived.

a) reads

b) is reading

c) read

d) has read

Jawaban: b) is reading

Soal 6:

Choose the correct word order for the sentence:

carrots / do / like / you / ?

a) You do like carrots?

b) Do you like carrots?

c) Carrots you do like?

d) Do like you carrots?

Jawaban: b) Do you like carrots?

Soal 7:

Identify the correct spelling of the word:

a) beutiful

b) beutifull

c) beautiful

d) beautifull

Jawaban: c) beautiful

Soal 8:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

The students ________ to the teacher's instructions.

a) listens

b) listen

c) listened

d) listening

Jawaban: b) listen

Soal 9:

Choose the correct possessive form of the noun:

The dog is ________ bone.

a) its

b) it's

c) their

d) they're

Jawaban: a) its

Soal 10:

Identify the correct punctuation for the sentence:

My favorite color is blue ________.

a) .

b) ,

c) !

d) ?

Jawaban: a) .

Semoga soal-soal ini bermanfaat untuk latihan bahasa Inggris Anda!

13. buku bahasa inggris bab 4 kelas 7 halaman 68 ktsp 2013


Direction menggunakan konjungsi untuk mendeskripsikan location, seperti

1. in front of = di depan

2. behind = di belakang

3. on the right = di sebelah kanan

4. on the left = di sebelah kiri

5. between = di antara

6. next to = di sebelah

7. across from = di seberang

8. on the corner = di pojok

Pembahasan

1. the bank is on the left, next to the post office, across from the tax office.

(bank di sebelah kiri kantor pos, di sebelah kantor pos, di seberang kantor pajak.)

2. the post office is next to the bank, across from the tax office.

(kantor pos di sebelah bank, di seberang kantor pajak)

3. The tax office is on the corner, next to the bank, across from the post office.

(Kantor pajak di pojok, di sebelah bank, di seberang kantor pos)

4. The school is on the right, next to the post office, across from the police station.

(sekolah di sebelah kanan, di sebelah kantor pos, di seberang kantor polisi).

5. The hospital is on the left, in front of police station, across from the post office.

(Rumah sakit di sebelah kiri, di depan kantor polisi, di seberang kantor pos)

6. The police station is on the left, across from hospital)

(kantor polisi di sebelah kiri, di seberang rumah sakit)

c. ∜16, 5√243, ∛125

d. ∜16, ∛125, 5√243

∛125 = 5

5√243 = 3

∜16 = 2

Jadi susunan bilangan dari terkecil adalah ∜16, 5√243, ∛125

S= 6 km
V= 24 km/jam
Berangkat pk.06.10
