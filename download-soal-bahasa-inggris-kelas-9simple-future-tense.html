Rumus Simple Future Tense
1. Positif
Subject + will/shall + verb I + object
Subject + am/is/are + going to + verb I + object
Contoh :
We will go to school soon
The students are going to have a test next month
2. Negatif
Subject + will/shall + verb I + object
Subject + am/is/are + not + going to + verb I + object
Contoh :
We will not go to school soon
The students are not going to have a test next
3. Pertanyaan
Will/shall + subject + verb I + object
Am/is/are + subject + going to + verb I + object
Contoh :
Will we go to school soon ?

16. tuliskan dan jelaskan perbedaan antara future continuous tense Dan simple present tense. ( tuliskan dalam bahasa Inggris)​


jawaban:

1. SIMPLE PRESENT

Simple Present Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu kegiatan atau peristiwa yang terjadi saat ini (present) dan juga untuk menyatakan kebenaran umum (fakta).

Rumus Present Tense ada 2 yaitu

a) VERBAL SENTENCE

= kalimat ang mengandung kata kerja (verb)

(+) S + VERB 1 (-s/es) + O

= I + GO + TO SCHOOL

(-) S + DO/DOES + NOT VERB 1 + O

= I + DO + NOT + GO + TO SCHOOL

(?) DO/DOES + S + VERB 1 + O

= DO + I + GO + TO SCHOOL?

b) NOMINAL SENTENCE

= Kalimat yg tidak ada kata kerja (verb)

(+) S + TO BE (IS/AM/ARE) + O

= I + AM + HAPPY

(-) S + TO BE (IS/AM/ARE) + NOT + O

= I + AM + NOT + HAPPY

(?) TO BE (IS/AM/ARE) + S + O?

= AM + I + HAPPY?

2. PRESENT CONTINUOUS

Present Continuous Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu kegiatan atau peristiwa yang saat ini (present) sedang berlangsung (continuous).

(+) S + TO BE (IS/AM/ARE) + VERB+ING + O

= I + AM + GOING + TO SCHOOL

(-) S + TO BE (IS/AM/ARE) + NOT + O

= I + AM + NOT + GOING + TO SCHOOL

(?) TO BE (IS/AM/ARE) + S + O?

= AM + I + GOING + TO SCHOOL?

3. SIMPLE PAST

Simple Past Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu kegiatan atau peristiwa yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau (past)

Rumus Present Tense ada 2 yaitu

a) VERBAL SENTENCE

= kalimat ang mengandung kata kerja (verb)

(+) S + VERB 1 (-s/es) + O

= I + WENT + TO SCHOOL

(-) S + DID + NOT VERB 1 + O

= I + DID + NOT + GO + TO SCHOOL

(?) DID + S + VERB 1 + O

= DID + I + GO + TO SCHOOL?

b) NOMINAL SENTENCE

= Kalimat yg tidak ada kata kerja (verb)

(+) S + TO BE (WAS/WERE) + O

= I + WAS + HAPPY

(-) S + TO BE (WAS/WERE) + NOT + O

= I + WAS + NOT + HAPPY

(?) TO BE (WAS/WERE) + S + O?

= WAS + I + HAPPY?

4. PAST CONTINUOUS

Past Continuous Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu kegiatan atau peristiwa yang sedang berlangsung (continuous) di masa lampau (past)

(+) S + TO BE (WAS/WERE) + VERB+ING + O

= I + WAS + GOING + TO SCHOOL

(-) S + TO BE (WAS/WERE) + NOT + O

= I + WAS + NOT + GOING + TO SCHOOL

(?) TO BE (WAS/WERE) + S + O?

= WAS + I + GOING + TO SCHOOL?

5. PRESENT PERFECT

Present Perfect Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu kegiatan atau peristiwa yang selesai sempurna (perfect) tapi ada kaitannya dengan saat ini (present).

(+) S + HAS/HAVE + VERB 3 + O

= I + HAVE + GONE + TO SCHOOL

(-) S + HAS/HAVE + NOT + VERB 3 + O

= I + HAVE + NOT + GONE + TO SCHOOL

(?) HAS/HAVE + S + VERB 3 + O

= HAVE + I + GONE + TO SCHOOL?

6. PAST PERFECT

Past Perfect Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu kegiatan atau peristiwa yang selesai sempurna (perfect) di masa lampau (past).

(+) S + HAD + VERB 3 + O

= I + HAD + GONE + TO SCHOOL

(-) S + HAD + NOT + VERB 3 + O

= I + HAD + NOT + GONE + TO SCHOOL

(?) HAD + S + VERB 3 + O

= HAD + I + GONE + TO SCHOOL?

7. SIMPLE FUTURE

Past Perfect Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan atau peristiwa yang akan (will) terjadi di masa yang akan datang (future).

(+) S + WILL/SHALL + VERB 1 + O

= I + WILL + GO + TO SCHOOL

(-) S + WILL/SHALL + NOT + VERB 1 + O

= I + WILL + NOT + GO + TO SCHOOL

(?) WILL/SHALL + S + VERB 1 + O

= WILL + I + GO + TO SCHOOL?

8. FUTURE BE GOING TO

Past Perfect Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan atau peristiwa yang akan (GOING TO) terjadi di masa yang akan datang (future).

(+) S + TO BE (IS/AM/ARE) + GOING TO + VERB 1 + O

= I + AM + GOING TO + GO + TO SCHOOL

(-) S + TO BE (IS/AM/ARE) + NOT + GOING TO + VERB 1 + O

= I + AM + NOT + GOING TO + GO + TO SCHOOL

(?) TO BE (IS/AM/ARE) + S + GOING TO + VERB 1 + O

= AM + I + GOING TO + GO + TO SCHOOL?

9. MODAL

Modal digunakan untuk menyatakan kemampuan (can) ketidakmampuan (can not/can't) atau perintah (must) dan larangan (must not/mustn't)

(+) S + CAN/MUST + VERB 1 + O

= I + CAN + DO + IT

(-) S + CAN/MUST + NOT + VERB 1 + O

= I + CAN + NOT + DO + IT

(?) CAN/MUST + S + VERB 1 + O?

+ CAN + I + DO + IT?

10. MODAL PREFECT

Perfect modal digunakan untuk menyatatakah hal yang bisa dilakukan di masa lalu tapi tidak dilakukan karena tidak berhasil dilakukan atau dengan kata lain suatu kegiatan yang seharusnya terjadi.

(+) S + COULD/SHOULD + HAVE + VERB 3 + O

= I + SHOULD + HAVE + BOUGHT + THE CAR

(-) S + COULD/SHOULD + NOT + HAVE + VERB 3 + O

= I + SHOULD + HAVE + NOT + BOUGHT + THE CAR

(?) COULD/SHOULD + S + HAVE + VERB 3 + O ?

= SHOULD + I + HAVE + BOUGHT + THE CAR?

21. future continuous tense dalam bahasa inggris dinyatakan dalam rumus


Future continuous tense atau future progressive tense rumusnya adalah sebagai berikut : 

Positive (+) S + will be + verb-ing(atau present participle) +..... .
Contohnya : I will be attending a party tomorrow.

Negative (-) S + will not be + verb-ing(atau present participle) +..... .
Contohnya : I will not be attending a party tomorrow.

Interrogative (?) Will + S + be + verb-ing(atau present participle) + .... ?
Contohnya : Will I be attending a party tomorrow?

27. perbedaan antara present tense past tense and future tense dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

if the present tense is used for events that are usually done or can be called 'habits', if past tense is used for activities that have occurred in the past,

while future tense is used for something that has not yet happened but has been planned, so you could say ideals.

Jawaban:

simple present tense

Used to show facts, habits, and general conditions that occur at this time.

formula: S + V / -s, es + complement

The time signal word for this tense is usually, always, every, sometimes, normally, seldom, etc.

Simple Past Tense

Used to indicate an event in the past.

formula: S + V2 + complement

The said time signals for this tense are last year, last month, yesterday, in 1996, last Sunday, etc.

Simple Future Tense

Used to indicate an action or a situation in the future.

formula: S + will / be going to + V1 + complement

The said time signal for this tense is next, tomorrow, five days later, etc.

29. tolong buatkan cerita bahasa inggris yang mengandung simple future tense,simple past future tense,dan present continous tense


Explanation:

- Simple future tenseThis tense is used to tell about an action that will be happened in the future.The structure: S + will + V1 + O
For example, I will go to Bali next month.

- Simple past future tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which would be happened in the past. This tense is mostly used to show wish or hope.
The structure: S + would + V1 + O
For example, I wish he would forgive me.

- Present continuous tenseThis tense is used to tell about something that is in progress right now.
The structure: S + to be (am / is / are) + V-ing + O
For example, She is reading a book in the library.

TOKOH: Cahaya & Bulan

ALUR CERITA: Bulan bercerita tentang kegiatan yang ia lakukan pada akhir minggu lalu kepada sahabatnya, Cahaya. Berkunjung ke rumah nenek di desa. Kegiatan di peternakan menyenangkan. Cahaya : Hi, Bulan. What did you do last weekend? (Hi Bulan, Apa yang kau lakukan akhir minggu lalu?)

Bulan : Oh, I did a lot of things. I went to a village last Saturday. (Oh, banyak hal kulakukan. Aku pergi ke desa pada hari Sabtu.)

Cahaya : Who did you visit? (Siapa yang kau kunjungi?)

Bulan : I visited my grandparents. They live in the village. (Aku mengunjungi kakek-nenek. Mereka tinggal di desa.)

Cahaya : Oh, I see. Who did you go with? (Oh, aku mengerti. Sama siapa kau pergi?)

Bulan : I went there with all of my family. They were mother, father and my younger sisters. (Aku pergi sama keluarga. Ada ibu, ayah dan adik-adikku.)

Cahaya : Wow, that would be so fun! (Wow, tentu menyenangkan!)

Bulan : Yeah, it was so fun. And we also felt tired. (Ya, menyenangkan dan kami juga merasa lelah.)

Cahaya : What did you do there? (Apa yang kau lakukan di sana?)

Bulan : We helped our grandparents in the farm. My sisters and I fed the cows, chicken, pigs and horses. Then we took some chicken eggs and gave them to mother and grandmother. They cooked our lunch. (Kami membantu kakek-nenek di peternakan. Adik-adik dan aku memberi makan sapi, ayam, babi dan kuda. Kemudian kami mengambil beberapa telur ayam dan memberikan kepada ibu & nenek. Mereka memasak makan siang.)

Cahaya : Did you ride a horse? (Apa kau berkuda?)

Bulan : Yes, we did. My sisters were really excited to ride a horse. They weren’t afraid at all. (Ya, kami berkuda. Adik-adikku sangat bersemangat. Mereka tidak takut sama sekali.)

Cahaya : What about you? (Kau gimana?)

Bulan : Of course I rode a horse too. (Tentu saja aku ikut berkuda.)

Cahaya : No, that’s not my question. My question is: Were you afraid at that time? (Bukan itu pertanyaanku. Pertanyaanku: waktu itu kau takut?)

Bulan : Me? Ha ha! Of course… I was … a little bit afraid. (Aku? Ha ha! Tentu saja aku … sedikit takut.)

Cahaya : Ha ha! I knew it. (Haha! Sudah kuduga.)

Cahaya : Nothing special. I didn’t do any fun activities like you. I was just at home on Saturday. And yeah, on Sunday I went fishing with my dad. (Nggak ada yang spesial. Aku nggak melakukan kegiatan menyenangkan sepertimu. Aku di rumah saja hari Sabtu. Dan ya, hari Senin aku mancing sama ayah.)

Bulan : How many fish did you get? (Berapa banyak ikan kau peroleh?)

Cahaya : We got only some fish because we had to finish it earlier. (Hanya beberapa saja karena kau harus selesai lebih awal.)

Bulan : Oh, why? (Oh, kenapa?)

Cahaya : Because my dad’s friend called him. He had to go and meet his friends. He forgot that he had had a promise to his friends. (Karena teman ayahku meneleponnya. Ia harus pergi dan ketemu mereka. Ia lupa kalau ada janji dengan teman-temannya.)

Bulan : Oh… don’t be sad. There will be other weekends, right? I hope you will have fun things to do next weekend. (Oh, jangan sedih. Akan ada akhir pekan selanjutnya, ya kan? Aku harap ada hal menyenangkan akhir pekan nanti.)

Cahaya : Yeah, I hope so. Thank you. (Ya, aku harap begitu. Makasih ya.)

Bulan : You are welcome. (Sama-sama.)
