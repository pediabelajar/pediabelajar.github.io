Simple past future tense

Adalah sebuah tenses yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan perisitiwa yang akan terjadi di masa yang akan datang, Pada saat lampau.

biasanya diikuti dengan kalimat pastens. dan terdiri dari dua tipe yaitu pola kalimat VERB dn pola kalimat NOMINAL

• Verb

pola :
S + Would/should + Verb 1 + object + adverb

Yesterday, when you visited My Uncle, He Would teach childrens In Papua.

• Nominal

Adalah sebuah pola yang bukan merupakan Kata Kerja. dan dapat mengungkapkan sebuah cita -cita.

pola :
S + would + be + Noun

Contoh :

• when you showed me our teacher profile last month, I think, I would be a teacher

• I would be a doctor in Hasanuddin university last year

22. pengertian Future Continuous tenses dalam bahasa inggris


The future continuous tense, sometimes also referred to as the future progressive tense, is a verb tense that indicates that something will occur in the future and continue for an expected length of time.

It is formed using the construction
: will + be + base verb+ing.

example: I will be cycling to school.

source: www.grammarly.com

23. Apa kegunaan Past future tense dalam bahasa inggris


Simple Past Future Tense dibentuk dengan dua tipe, pertama yaitu ‘would’ dan bare infinitive (bentuk simpel dari verb). Tipe kedua dibentuk dari ‘was/were’, present participle ‘going’ dan infinitive. Infinitive yaitu suatu kata yang terdiri dari to + bare infinitive. Tipe kedua lebih sering digunakan untuk menyatakan aksi yang telah direncanakan.

29. future continuous tense dalam bahasa inggris dinyatakan dalam rumus


Future continuous tense atau future progressive tense rumusnya adalah sebagai berikut : 

Positive (+) S + will be + verb-ing(atau present participle) +..... .
Contohnya : I will be attending a party tomorrow.

Negative (-) S + will not be + verb-ing(atau present participle) +..... .
Contohnya : I will not be attending a party tomorrow.

Interrogative (?) Will + S + be + verb-ing(atau present participle) + .... ?
Contohnya : Will I be attending a party tomorrow?

40. Function dari simple future, future continous, future perfect ? Jawabnya jangan asal-asalan, pake bahasa inggris


function of simple future is to tell someone about an activity that relates with future action. example given is SHE WILL STUDY TOMORROW (dia akan belajar besok)
function of future continuous is to tell someone about an activity that relates with future action and is about to be in progress, example given is SHE WILL BE COOKING TOMORROW (dia akan sedang masak besok) 
function of future perfect is to tell someone about an activity that relates with future action and is about to complete/finish at a certain time, example given SHE WILL HAVE COME (dia akan sudah datang/akan hampir datang) - Function of Simple Future tense is used to express events or actions that will be done or will be occur in the future.

- Function of Future Continuous tense is used to express actions or event that to be going on at a certain time in the future. But, this actions has been started but not yet completed at that time.

-Function of Future perfect tense is used to express that an actions will have finished in the future..
